Features

Everything Scythe adds to the FFXI experience

The NM Hunt

475 regular NMs across 146 zones. Unique mythological names, distinct models, Level 100 with 10x stat multipliers. 9-11 min respawn. Scyld currency on kill. Progressive elite system gated behind full completion.

Endgame

Elite Hunts

25 legendary bosses gated behind 475/475 completion. Story characters as bosses with custom dialogue and weaponskills. Linear progression to The Time Devourer.

Ultimate Challenge

REMA Custom Mods

All 14 relics rebalanced: fTP 5.0/9.0/13.0, multi-hit fTP replication, 35% OCC 3x. Full stats table for every relic WS. Empyrean aftermath procs on WSes.

Balance

Weapon Progression

Streamlined paths to Relic, Mythic, Empyrean, Aeonic, and Prime weapons. Adjusted requirements plus hunt shortcut via Kupon W-RMEA.

Weapons

Scyld Reward Shop

Spend hunt currency on monster suits, event gear, anniversary sets, and milestone Mog Kupons. Final reward: any RMEA weapon at 500 unique kills.

Rewards

Missions & Story

Every expansion mission line through Rhapsodies fully implemented. Mission skip NPC. Magian Trials enabled (streamlined kill counts). Blue Magic fully implemented (67+ spells).

Story

Empress's Repair Decree

Server-wide crafting event in Aht Urhgan Whitegate. Donate signed self-crafted materials to 8 guilds for GP rewards. 45-minute window, twice weekly.

Crafting

Trust Guide

Complete guide to every Trust available through Login Points. Roles, jobs, abilities, upgrades, and team-building tips. Individual pages for every trust with beginner-friendly explanations.

Guide

Login Points

Permanent Login Campaign. Earn 500 points on your first login each day, 100 on each after. Spend on Trust Ciphers, mounts, rings, and more from the Greeter Moogle.

Daily

More Features & QoL

Ferry pirates, campaign battles, wedding ceremony, Abyssea boosted NMs, Chocobo raising, Omen, 30+ QoL improvements, custom commands, and more.
