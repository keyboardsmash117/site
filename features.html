Features

Everything Scythe adds to the FFXI experience

The NM Hunt

475 regular NMs across 150 zones. Unique mythological names, distinct models, Level 160 with 10x stat multipliers. 9-11 min respawn. Scyld currency on kill.

Endgame

Elite Hunts

25 legendary bosses gated behind 475/475 completion. Story characters as bosses with custom dialogue and weaponskills. Linear progression to The Time Devourer.

Ultimate Challenge

The Colosseum

7 tiers of timed boss fights (Bronze through Adamant). 5 challenge modes: Normal, Hard, Speed Kill, Solo, No Magic. Gauntlet mode. Exemplar Points for Master Levels.

Arena

Master Levels

50 master levels earned through Colosseum EP. Per-job tracking. Higher ML unlocks harder tiers up to Adamant (ML 50, 8M HP bosses).

Progression

Siege Raids

Server-wide 5-wave raid events with FFXIV-style mechanics. Vulnerability windows, boss AI phases, visual callouts. EP rewards.

Raid

REMA Custom Mods

All 14 relics rebalanced: fTP 5.0/9.0/13.0, multi-hit fTP replication, 35% OCC 3x. Full stats table for every relic WS. Empyrean aftermath procs on WSes.

Balance

Dynamis Divergence

4 instanced high-difficulty Dynamis zones: Bastok, San d'Oria, Windurst, Jeuno. Custom mob pools and droplists.

Group Content

Fay Weapon Augments

20 augmentable weapons across 14 Shadowreign zones. Random augments with up to 5 reroll attempts. One weapon per job.

Quest

Weapon Progression

Streamlined paths to Relic, Mythic, Empyrean, Aeonic, and Prime weapons. Adjusted requirements plus hunt shortcut via Kupon W-RMEA.

Weapons

Scyld Reward Shop

Spend hunt currency on monster suits, event gear, anniversary sets, and milestone Mog Kupons. Final reward: any RMEA weapon at 500 unique kills.

Rewards

Missions & Story

Every expansion mission line through Rhapsodies fully implemented. Mission skip NPC. Magian Trials enabled.

Story

Other Features

Ferry pirates, Campaign battles, and more custom content.
