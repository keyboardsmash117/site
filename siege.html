Siege Raids

Server-wide 5-wave raid events

Server-wide 5-wave raid events with FFXIV-inspired mechanics. When a siege starts, the whole server is notified — anyone who wants to participate converges on the battle zone to fight escalating waves of enemies, culminating in a phased boss. Siege is currently the primary live source of Exemplar Points for Master Level progression.

How to join a siege
  1. Watch for the announcement. When a siege begins, every online player receives a linkshell-chat notification and on-screen callout naming the zone.
  2. Warp in. Type !headto and pick the city nearest the siege zone, or use your home-point / Warp spell. The siege zone stays open for the duration of the event.
  3. Engage. Join any existing party or fight solo — EP is tracked per-player, so you don't need a fixed group. Trusts scale up for siege and can fill gaps in a small party.
  4. Survive the 5 waves. Each wave is harder than the last. Waves 1–3 are the approach, wave 4 is a gauntlet, wave 5 is the boss.
  5. Collect rewards on completion. EP is awarded proportional to contribution, with a baseline for anyone who stays through the end.

What's Different About Siege Bosses

  • Phase-based AI — the boss moves through distinct combat phases. Each phase changes the ability rotation and enrage timer.
  • Vulnerability windows — the boss takes reduced damage by default. Vulnerability opens during specific mechanic windows; hit hard when you see the callout. Rewards coordinated burst over steady DPS.
  • Custom siege abilities & spells — mechanics that only exist in siege (telegraphed AoEs, mobile stacks, role-tagged debuffs).
  • On-screen callouts — visual cues announce every major mechanic. No addon required.
  • Ability synergy rewards — normally underused job abilities (Cover, Rampart, Palisade, Sentinel, Majesty, etc.) gain special effects during siege mechanics. Using the right ability at the right moment is how mitigation actually works.
