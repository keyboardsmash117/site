Siege Raids

Server-wide 5-wave raid events

Server-wide 5-wave raid events with FFXIV-inspired mechanics. When a siege begins, players converge on the battle zone to defend against escalating waves of enemies culminating in a boss fight.

  • 5 waves — escalating difficulty with increasing mob count and strength per wave
  • Boss AI phases — the siege boss has distinct combat phases with different abilities, vulnerability windows, and mechanics
  • Vulnerability system — boss takes reduced damage outside of vulnerability windows, rewarding coordinated burst damage
  • Siege abilities and spells — custom mechanics specific to siege encounters
  • Visual callouts — on-screen indicators for mechanics
  • Exemplar Points — siege participation awards EP toward master levels
