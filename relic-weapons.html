Relic Weapons Reborn

All 14 relics rebalanced for endgame

All 14 relic weapons have been rebalanced to be genuinely endgame-competitive. The numbers:

  • fTP 5.0 / 9.0 / 13.0 — every relic weaponskill scales aggressively with TP
  • Multi-hit fTP replication — all hits get the full fTP value, not just the first. This is what makes them competitive with empyrean and mythic WSes.
  • Occasionally Attacks 3 Times — every relic 119-III weapon has a 50% chance to triple its hits
  • Ranged relics — Coronach and Namas Arrow have 2 base hits
  • H2H exception — Final Heaven has 2 hits but no fTP replication (intentional balance)

All 14 relics: Mandau, Excalibur, Ragnarok, Guttler, Bravura, Gungnir, Apocalypse, Kikoku, Amanomurakumo, Mjollnir, Claustrum, Yoichinoyumi, Annihilator, Spharai.
