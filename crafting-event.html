Empress's Repair Decree

Server-wide crafting event

Overview

By order of Empress Nashmeira II, all skilled craftsmen are called to aid in Al Zahbi's restoration. The Imperial Herald appears in Aht Urhgan Whitegate twice per week, issuing an emergency repair decree for the city's defenses.

DetailValue
LocationAht Urhgan Whitegate (Imperial Herald NPC)
Duration45 minutes per event
FrequencyTwice weekly (every 3.5 days)
AnnouncementServer-wide at 30, 15, 5, and 1 minute before start
RewardGuild Points (GP) per accepted item

How It Works

Each event, one item is chosen at random from each guild's pool. The Imperial Herald posts a list of required materials at the start of the decree.

Donate items you have crafted yourself (signed with your character name) to earn guild points. The Empire only accepts materials wrought by the artisan who presents them — purchased items are rejected.

HQ crafted items earn a 50% GP bonus. Server-wide milestone announcements fire at 50, 100, and 200 items donated per guild.

Tips

  • Watch for the server announcement — the event starts with a 1-minute warning.
  • Check the Imperial Herald immediately on arrival to see which items are needed this event.
  • HQ items always yield 50% more GP. Worth the extra synth attempt if your skill is high enough.
  • Items must be signed by your own character. Pre-crafting before the event starts is the most efficient approach.
  • Each item donated counts toward guild milestones. Server announcements fire at 50, 100, and 200 per guild.
