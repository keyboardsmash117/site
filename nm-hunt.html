The NM Hunt

500 hunt NMs — 475 regular + 25 elite across 146 zones

Regular Hunt NMs

Kill trackingPer-character, unique kills only
  • Unique names — drawn from real-world mythology and folklore. Chelona, Nuckelavee, Girtablilu, Lamashtu, Brollachan. No generic names, no duplicates.
  • Distinct models — every NM uses a unique model. No two share the same appearance.
  • Dynamic entity spawning — NMs appear in the overworld with no instancing. Walk up and fight them.
  • Scyld currency — every kill awards Scyld to your party. Spend it at the Hunts Master NPC.

Daily & Weekly Bounties

The Hunts Master assigns bonus bounties that reset on a timer. Each bounty targets a specific NM — kill it to earn bonus Scyld on top of the normal per-kill reward. Each player's assigned NM is unique to them.

Elite Hunt NMs

Kill all 475 regular NMs to unlock the elite hunt system. Elite NMs use a strict progressive gating system — there is no skipping tiers. You must clear lower tiers before higher tiers will spawn. Each elite also has its own individual prereqs (prior kills, weather, time of day, moon phase, player titles) that must be satisfied.

Elemental Gyves

The 8 Elemental Seals are the entry point into the elite system. Each is bound to a specific zone. Kill the matching elemental family in that zone to build up your kill counter, then wait for the right weather to force the spawn.

Wind SealMamookAir Elemental / PukWind / Gales

Clearing Gyves unlocks the Humanoid Champions, each with unique spawn conditions (Lightsday + dawn, full moon + dusk, solo player + Windsday, etc.). Champions unlock the Special Bosses and Supreme Beings. Clear all field elites to face Absolute Virtue. Time Devourer is a separate BCNM encounter.
