The NM Hunt

475 regular NMs + 25 elite bosses across 150 zones

The core of Scythe's endgame. 475 regular hunt NMs spread across 150 zones, plus 25 elite bosses gated behind full completion. Every NM is built from scratch.

  • Unique names — drawn from real-world mythology and folklore across dozens of cultures. Chelona, Nuckelavee, Girtablilu, Pandaemonium, Lamashtu. No generic names, no duplicates.
  • Distinct models — every NM has a unique visual appearance. No two NMs share the same model.
  • Level 160 with 10x stat multipliers — they hit hard, have real HP pools, and use actual mob skill lists with proper AI. Some have AV-style 2-hour cycling and phase transitions.
  • Scyld currency — every kill awards Scyld. Spend it at the Hunts Master.
  • Per-character kill tracking — every unique NM you kill is recorded. Milestones at every 50 kills. Kill all 475 to unlock elite hunts.
  • 9-11 minute respawn — no waiting hours. If someone gets there first, come back in 10 minutes.
  • 150 zones — NMs are distributed across the entire world, from starter zones to Abyssea to Seekers of Adoulin areas. Every zone has 3 NMs.
  • Dynamic entity spawning — NMs appear in the overworld with no instancing required. Walk up and fight them.
