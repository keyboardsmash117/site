Missions & Story Content

Every expansion fully playable

  • All Missions Implemented — every expansion mission line is fully implemented and playable from start to finish. Zilart, CoP, ToAU, WotG, Adoulin, Rhapsodies — all complete with proper cutscenes, battlefields, and rewards.
  • Mission Skip — already cleared an expansion's story on retail? Skip completed mission lines and jump straight to endgame content. No replaying hours of cutscenes you've already seen.
  • Magian Trials Adjusted — streamlined trial paths with adjusted requirements. Still meaningful progression, less tedious grinding.
