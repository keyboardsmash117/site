Missions & Story Content

Every expansion fully playable

  • All Missions Implemented — every expansion mission line is fully implemented and playable from start to finish. Zilart, CoP, ToAU, WotG, Adoulin, Rhapsodies of Vana'diel — all complete with proper cutscenes, battlefields, and rewards.
  • Mission Skip — already cleared an expansion's story on retail? Skip completed mission lines and jump straight to endgame content. No replaying hours of cutscenes you've already seen.
  • Magian Trials — enabled with streamlined kill counts and adjusted conditions. Magian trial items available through standard quest NPCs.
  • Blue Magic — fully implemented with 67+ spells. Proper damage formulas (alpha/WSC scaling), stat-based damage calculations, and all blue magic set point bonuses.
  • SOA Missions — Seekers of Adoulin story accessible without imprimatur requirements. WotG story missions are not gated behind nation quests.
