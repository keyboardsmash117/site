The Scyld Reward Shop

Spend your hunt currency on rewards

Every hunt NM kill awards Scyld — the hunt currency. 25 per regular NM kill (shared to every party member), 250 per elite kill, plus 50–500 bonus Scyld when you complete the right daily/hard/weekly bounty. Spend Scyld at the NM Hunt shop NPC, right next to the Hunts Master in Celennia Memorial Library.

Two things to spend it on: pure-cosmetic gear (monster suits, event gear, anniversary sets) and milestone Kupons — Mog Kupons redeemable for real endgame rewards as you hit unique-kill thresholds.

How to shop
  1. Accumulate Scyld by killing hunt NMs.
  2. Enter Celennia Memorial Library via Eppel-Treppel · Eastern Adoulin.
  3. Talk to NM Hunt · Celennia Memorial Library — the shop NPC, directly next to the Hunts Master.
  4. Browse the three cosmetic pages, or redeem a milestone Kupon if you're eligible.
  5. Milestone Kupons are item vouchers. Trade them to the appropriate Mog Bonanza Moogle (any starting city) to redeem the actual reward (weapon / armor piece / seal).

Milestone Rewards

Milestone rewards unlock as you hit unique NM kill thresholds. Each reward is a Mog Kupon — redeem it for powerful gear and upgrades. The 50-kill reward is auto-awarded the moment you cross the threshold; 100+ rewards you buy from the shop once eligible.

AF / Relic / Emp
The three classic job-specific armor sets from retail FFXI — Artifact / Relic / Empyrean. The I-AF119 and I-Seal kupons upgrade these.
Abdhaljs seal
Upgrade item for +2 / +3 armor tiers. Normally from Escha/Omen content — I-Seal lets you get one straight from hunt progress.
Ambuscade (AW)
Retail's monthly weapon/armor event. AW-WK, AW-UWIII, and AW-GFIII kupons give Ambuscade-equivalent gear from WK Reive / Unity / Geas Fete.
Unity (UW)
Unity Concord weapons from retail. AW-UWIII gives a Tier-III version.
Wildskeeper Reive (WK)
Outdoor mini-raids from Seekers of Adoulin. AW-WK gives one WK Reive weapon.
Geas Fete (GF)
Reisenjima outdoor NM series. AW-GFIII gives a Tier-III GF armor piece.
REMA / RMEA
Relic / Mythic / Empyrean / Aeonic — the four endgame weapon categories. I-RME gives 300 upgrade materials (enough for one weapon upgrade path). W-RMEA at 500 kills = one completely free REMA weapon at 119-III, fully upgraded.
