The Scyld Reward Shop

Spend your hunt currency on rewards

Every hunt NM kill awards Scyld — the hunt currency. Spend it at the NM Hunt NPC in Celennia Memorial Library. The shop has cosmetic collectibles and a milestone reward track that scales with your hunt progress.

Milestone Rewards

Milestone rewards unlock as you hit unique NM kill thresholds. Each reward is a Mog Kupon — redeem it for powerful gear and upgrades. The 50-kill reward is auto-awarded on kill; 100+ require Scyld purchase.

The final milestone — Kupon W-RMEA at 500 unique kills — lets you choose any Relic, Mythic, Empyrean, or Aeonic weapon. This is the ultimate reward for completing the entire hunt system.
