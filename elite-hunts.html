Elite Hunts

Progressive gated bosses — no skipping

Overview

Kill all 475 regular hunt NMs and the elite system unlocks. Elite NMs are characters drawn from FFXI's own story — reimagined as open-world endgame encounters with custom dialogue, spawn conditions, and AI.

There is no skipping. The tiers must be cleared in sequence. You cannot access Humanoid Champions until you've cleared the Elemental Gyves. You cannot reach the Special Bosses without clearing the Champions. Every elite also has its own individual prerequisites — time of day, weather, moon phase, player titles, kill counts in the zone — on top of the tier requirement.

Final — Absolute Virtue

Clear all field elites and speak to the Hunts Master to spawn Absolute Virtue in Mount Kamihr. AV does not auto-spawn — it is a deliberate act. It uses every job ability in the game on rotating cooldowns.

Killing Absolute Virtue resets the entire elite progression. The Seals, the Champions, the Special Bosses, the Supreme Beings — everything clears. You start over.
