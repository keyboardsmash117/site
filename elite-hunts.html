Elite Hunts

25 legendary bosses — the ultimate challenge

Kill all 475 regular hunt NMs and the real challenge begins. 25 legendary bosses — characters drawn from FFXI's own story, reimagined as endgame encounters.

  • Linear progression — elites must be cleared in order. Each one unlocks the next. Difficulty escalates from serious to punishing.
  • Story characters as bosses — Excenmille, Robel-Akbel, Perih Vashai, Prishe, Tenzen, Iroha, Kamlanaut, Balamor, and more. Each has custom dialogue, personality, and weaponskills.
  • Spawn conditions — some require specific weather, day of the week, nation control, or pre-kill requirements (e.g., kill 50 Orcs in the zone before Excenmille will spawn).
  • Scyld cost — early elites cost 500-1,000. Mid-tier 2,000-5,000. The final boss costs 25,000.
  • 3 expansion tiers — distributed across FFXI's expansions (base game through RoV), each with an elemental seal and two named bosses.
  • The Time Devourer — elite #25. Requires all 24 previous elites cleared. Multi-phase, form-shifting. Clearing it means you've beaten Scythe.
