Server Rules

Keep it simple. Respect the game and respect each other.

General Conduct

  • Be respectful. No harassment, hate speech, slurs, or targeted abuse. Disagreements happen — handle them like adults.
  • No real-money trading (RMT). Selling in-game items, currency, or accounts for real money is an immediate permanent ban. No exceptions.
  • No cheating or exploits. Third-party tools that automate gameplay (bots), speed hacks, packet manipulation, or any exploit that gives an unfair advantage are prohibited. If you find a bug, report it.
  • One account per player. Multiboxing (running multiple characters simultaneously) is not permitted. One person, one character online at a time.
  • English in public channels. /shout and /yell should be in English so everyone can participate. Private conversations can be in any language.

Gameplay

  • NM claim rules. First to engage a Notorious Monster has claim. Do not intentionally MPK, train mobs onto other players, or interfere with someone else's fight.
  • No camping disruption. If someone is clearly camping an NM spawn, don't grief them. If there's a dispute, bring it to staff.
  • Report bugs, don't abuse them. If something gives unintended rewards, lets you skip content, or seems broken — report it in Discord. Players who abuse bugs instead of reporting them will face consequences.

Enforcement

  • Warnings first. Most first offenses get a warning. We're not looking for reasons to ban people.
  • Staff decisions are final. If you disagree with a ruling, you can appeal in Discord DMs with a staff member. Public arguments about moderation help nobody.
  • Zero tolerance for RMT and botting. These are the two things that get immediate permanent bans with no warning.
