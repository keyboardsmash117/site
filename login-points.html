Login Points

Permanent Login Campaign — earn points just by playing

How It Works

Scythe runs a permanent Login Campaign. Every day you log in, you earn points to spend on Trust Ciphers, mounts, rings, and other useful items from the Greeter Moogle.

New to Trusts? Check the Trust Guide for a full breakdown of every trust — what they do, which roles they fill, and how to build your party.

LoginPoints Earned
First login of the day500 points
Each additional daily login100 points

Points carry over each month (capped at 1,500 carried). There is no monthly reset — the campaign is always active.

Where to Spend

Talk to the Greeter Moogle in any of the three starting nations (South San d'Oria, Bastok Markets, Windurst Woods) to browse the prize list.
