Synergy

Advanced crafting via elemental fusion — live, available worldwide

Overview

Synergy is a Wings-of-the-Goddess crafting system that fuses materials using elemental energy instead of the standard single-craft skill-up flow. Instead of being tied to one of the 9 retail crafts, Synergy combines elemental "fewell" with catalyst materials at a Synergy Furnace, producing items you cannot make any other way — monster suits, HQ event gear, select weapons, and unique cosmetics.

On Scythe, Synergy is enabled and available in 17 zones covering every capital and major outpost. No guild rank requirement, no unlock quest — just find the nearest Synergy Engineer and start.

How to start
  1. Find a Synergy Engineer and a Synergy Furnace. Any of the 17 zones listed below. The Engineer teaches recipes and converts your crystals into fewell; the Furnace is the actual crafting device.
  2. Pick a recipe. The Engineer walks through what materials are needed. Synergy recipes require elemental fewell (made from crystals) plus the recipe's specific catalysts.
  3. Run the session at the Furnace. Synergy is an active mini-game — balance the elemental flow during the session. Higher instability risks destroying the materials.
  4. Skill-up as you go. Your Synergy skill rises with use, just like any other craft. Higher skill = more stable sessions and better HQ rates.
  5. Collect the output. Success produces one or more items; HQ produces the premium variant (e.g. +1 monster suits, HQ event gear).
