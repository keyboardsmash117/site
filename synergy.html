Synergy

Advanced crafting via elemental fusion

Overview

Synergy is a crafting system introduced in Wings of the Goddess that combines materials using elemental energy. Synergy Furnaces and Engineers are available in 17 zones across Vana'diel, including all major cities and Aht Urhgan Whitegate.

Synergy produces HQ versions of monster suits, event gear, select weapons, and other unique items not obtainable through standard crafting guilds.
