Community

Discord

The hub for everything Scythe. Get the server IP and connection info, find a group, ask questions, report bugs, and keep up with development updates. All announcements go here first.

Bug Reports

Found something broken? The #bug-reports channel in Discord is the fastest way to get it fixed. Include the zone name, what you were doing, what happened, and screenshots if possible.

New Player Help

Never played FFXI before? Returning after years away? The #new-player-guide channel has setup help, job recommendations, and veteran players happy to answer questions.

Discord Channels

Here's what you'll find when you join:

#connection-info

Server IP, port, and account registration instructions. This is where you go first to get connected.

#announcements

Server updates, patch notes, maintenance windows, and new content releases. Follow this channel to stay current.

#general

General chat about the server, FFXI, and whatever else. This is where the community hangs out.

#new-player-guide

Setup help, beginner tips, recommended jobs, leveling guides, and trust unlocks. Ask anything — no question is too basic.

#bug-reports

Found a bug? Report it here with zone, steps to reproduce, and what you expected to happen. Bug reporters are appreciated.

#nm-hunt

Discussion about the NM hunt system — strategies, locations, elite hunt tips, and kill tracking. Share your progress with other hunters.
