Coming Soon

Systems in active development. Partial implementations may exist on the server but are not yet released.

FATE Events

Open-world dynamic events that appear across the leveling path, modeled after FFXIV's FATE system. Two types: Annihilation (kill a target count of mobs in a timed window) and Notorious (take down one powerful field boss). FATEs auto-sync your level to the zone cap on first engage and track individual contribution — kill credits and time in the event area determine whether you earn Gold, Silver, or Bronze Fate Marks on completion. Events fire on a cooldown schedule zone by zone, covering zones from level 15 through the 40s.

The Colosseum

Timed arena boss fights across 5 tiers (Bronze → Mythril) with 5 modes (Normal, Hard, Speed Kill, Solo, No Magic) and a Gauntlet chain mode. Designed as the primary path for Exemplar Points. System design →

Ferry Pirates

Pirate crews will board the Selbina/Mhaura and Nashmau ferries with scaled mobs and linked aggro. The ride becomes a real event rather than a travel timer.

Chocobo Raising

The Chocobo raising / breeding mini-game. Raise a personal chocobo from egg to adult via daily care actions (feeding, grooming, training), with various colour and stat outcomes depending on parentage and care choices. Currently available for testing but not yet feature-complete.

Delve

The Seekers of Adoulin instanced content. Bosses, Plasm currency, segmented progression, and the associated gear sets — to be scoped and tuned to fit Scythe's endgame curve.

Omen

The Reisenjima Henge instanced content. Fight six Caturae field bosses (Fu, Kyou, Kei, Gin, Kin, Ou) and three Glassy mid-bosses (Craver, Gorger, Thinker), each with their own TP move set. Drops include Moonbow materials, scale currency, and rare body pieces. Instance implementation is complete; tuning and reward tables are still being finalized.

Sortie

A 60-minute instanced run through Outer Ra'Kaznar with eight sectors (A–H). Each sector has a minor NM and a major NM, culminating in the ultimate boss Aminon. Killing mobs drops gallimaufry currency used for rewards. Features a plate mechanic — your Sortie Plate degrades after each run and recharges over time (or faster with Ruspix's Plate). 17 NMs total.

Recently shipped: Synergy crafting is now live in 17 zones.

No ETAs. Development is ongoing and prioritized by the team. This list may change as systems ship or new ones enter planning.
