Features in development

These features are actively being worked on. Some may be partially implemented — you might see pieces of them in-game, but they are not yet polished or fully functional. They will be moved to the main features page once they are ready.

The Colosseum Partial

Timed arena boss fights with multiple difficulty tiers and challenge modes. Fight increasingly powerful bosses for Exemplar Points used toward Master Levels.

  • 7 planned tiers: Bronze, Iron, Mythril, Darksteel, Titanium, Orichalcum, Adamant
  • 5 challenge modes: Normal, Hard, Speed Kill, Solo, No Magic
  • Gauntlet mode — fight all bosses in a tier back-to-back
  • Exemplar Points (EP) earned per clear

Master Levels Partial

A progression system beyond level 99. Earn Master Levels through Exemplar Points from the Colosseum. Tied to the Colosseum system.

  • 50 master levels, tracked per-job
  • Higher ML unlocks harder Colosseum tiers
  • Adamant tier (ML 50) features bosses with 8M+ HP

Siege Raids Partial

Server-wide raid events inspired by FFXIV's FATE system. Large-scale battles with multi-phase bosses and mechanics that require coordination.

  • 5-wave format with escalating difficulty
  • FFXIV-style mechanics: vulnerability windows, visual callouts, boss phase transitions
  • EP rewards for participating

Dynamis Divergence Partial

High-difficulty instanced versions of the four Dynamis city zones with custom mob pools, unique droplists, and tougher encounters.

  • 4 zones: Bastok, San d'Oria, Windurst, Jeuno
  • Custom mob pools and AI
  • Unique droplists separate from regular Dynamis
  • Instanced — each group gets their own copy

Fay Weapon Augments Planned

A quest system where players earn augmentable weapons by completing tasks in Wings of the Goddess Shadowreign zones. Each weapon can be customized with random augments.

  • 20 weapons across 14 Shadowreign zones
  • Random augments with up to 5 reroll attempts
  • One weapon per job

Synergy Partial

A specialized crafting system separate from the standard 8 crafts. Uses Synergy Furnaces found in cities and field areas to create unique items.

  • Synergy Furnaces and Engineers planned for 17 zones
  • Unique recipes not available through standard crafting
  • Requires multiple players working together at the furnace

Ferry Pirates Partial

Pirate crews board the ferry routes between Mhaura and Selbina. Scaled mobs with linked aggro spawn on deck mid-voyage. Clear them before port.

Abyssea Boosted NMs Partial

Enhanced Abyssea Notorious Monsters with increased Atma and light drop rates (80% instead of retail). Makes Abyssea progression much faster.

Omen Partial

Omen objectives and content with custom global settings for the Escha system. Includes boss encounters and unique rewards.

Chocobo Raising QoL Planned

Quality of life improvements to the Chocobo raising system, making the process smoother and less tedious.

World First Announcements Partial

Server-wide broadcast when a player achieves a first on the server — first to clear content, first to obtain items, etc.
