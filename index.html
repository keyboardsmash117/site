02 —CUSTOM CONTENT

Eleven pieces of original endgame.

Everything below is built on top of the full FFXI game. Not a rebalance — new content, new systems, new reasons to stay logged in.

N°01 · FLAGSHIP

The NM Hunt

475 regular hunt NMs across 146 zones. Unique mythological names drawn from real folklore — Chelona, Nuckelavee, Girtablilu, Lamashtu — distinct models, hand-tuned AI. Level 100 with 10× stat multipliers. 9–11 minute respawn. 25 Scyld per kill, awarded to the party. Kill tracking is per-character, unique kills only.

Endgame
N°02

Elite Hunts

25 gated bosses drawn from FFXI's story. Unlocks after 475/475. Four tiers, strict order — Seals → Champions → Special → Supreme. Conditional spawns: weather, moon, day, titles, zone-state.

Ultimate
Coming Soon
N°03

The Colosseum

Timed boss fights in a custom arena zone. 5 tiers — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Mythril — with base EP rewards from 1,250 up to 125,000 per clear. 5 modes: Normal, Hard, Speed Kill, Solo, No Magic — each with its own EP multiplier. Gauntlet runs chain fights across tiers. Primary path for Exemplar Points.

Arena
N°04

Siege Raids

Server-wide 5-wave events with FFXIV-inspired mechanics. Boss AI phases, vulnerability windows, siege-specific abilities, on-screen callouts. Awards Exemplar Points on participation.

Raid
N°05

REMA Custom Mods

All 14 relics rebalanced at every tier (75 / 119 / 119-III). 35% OCC 3× on relic WS (Mandau 40%), with multi-hit fTP replication. Full stat tables on the mods page.

Balance
N°06

Dynamis Divergence

Instanced high-difficulty Dynamis. Four zones: Bastok, San d'Oria, Windurst, Jeuno. Custom mob pools and droplists.

Group
N°07

Master Levels

50 levels of post-cap progression, tracked per-job via Exemplar Points from the Colosseum. +1 to all stats, +7 HP, +2 MP per level. Retail EP curve — ML 50 costs ~3.3M EP on the final push.

Progression
N°08

Fay Weapon Augments

Succor to the Sidhe quest. Trade a Fay, Erlking, Dweomer, or Ogre weapon to Callisto in Grauberg [S], fight Pixie NMs across 14 Shadowreign zones, receive up to 2 random augments from that zone's pool. 20 weapons covering every job. 5 rerolls per weapon.

Quest
N°09

Scyld Reward Shop

Spend hunt currency at the NM Hunts NPC in Celennia Memorial Library. Monster suits, event gear, anniversary sets, milestone Mog Kupons — plus auto-awarded milestone rewards that scale with unique kills.

Rewards
N°10

All Missions Complete

Every expansion mission line through Rhapsodies fully implemented. Mission skip NPC available. Magian Trials enabled.

Story
N°11

QoL & More

30+ QoL improvements, Omen, Abyssea boosted NMs, wedding ceremony, Chocobo raising, custom commands. Ferry Pirates, Delve, and Synergy in active development.
