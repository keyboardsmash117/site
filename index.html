Full Server Details

Custom Content

Everything below is original content built on top of the full FFXI game.

The NM Hunt

475 regular hunt NMs across 150 zones + 25 elite bosses. Unique mythological names, distinct models, hand-tuned AI. Level 160 with stat multipliers. Respawn every 9-11 minutes. Earn Scyld currency on kill.

Endgame

Elite Hunts

Kill all 475 hunt NMs to unlock 25 legendary bosses. Linear progression, unique dialogue, custom weaponskills. The final boss costs 25,000 Scyld to attempt.

Ultimate Challenge

The Colosseum

Timed boss fights in a custom arena zone. 7 tiers (Bronze through Adamant, up to 8M HP). 5 challenge modes: Normal, Hard, Speed Kill, Solo, No Magic. Gauntlet mode for consecutive fights. Rewards Exemplar Points for Master Levels.

Arena

Siege Raids

5-wave raid events with FFXIV-style mechanics: vulnerability windows, boss AI phases, siege abilities and spells, visual callouts. Server-wide event with scaling difficulty.

Raid

Relic Weapons Reborn

All 14 relic weapons rebalanced. fTP 5.0/9.0/13.0, full multi-hit fTP replication, 50% chance triple damage (OCC 3x). Every relic is endgame competitive.

Balance

Dynamis Divergence

Instanced high-difficulty Dynamis. Four zones: Bastok, San d'Oria, Windurst, Jeuno. Custom mob pools, divergence-specific droplists.

Group Content

Master Levels

50 master levels earned through Colosseum fights. Tiers scale from Bronze (ML 0, 150K HP bosses) to Adamant (ML 50, 8M HP bosses). Each tier awards increasing Exemplar Points.

Progression

Fay Weapon Augments

Succor to the Sidhe quest system. Trade a Fay/Erlking weapon to Callisto, fight Pixie NMs across 14 Shadowreign zones, receive randomly augmented weapons. 20 weapons covering all jobs. Up to 5 reroll attempts.

Quest

Scyld Reward Shop

Spend Scyld from hunt kills on real rewards — including a full 119-III REMA weapon. The hunt currency has a meaningful endgame payoff.

Rewards

All Missions Complete

Every expansion mission line fully implemented. Mission skip available for completed content. Magian trials adjusted for streamlined progression.

Story

Ferry Pirates

Pirate crews board the ferries. Scaled mobs spawn on deck with linked aggro. Clear the boarding party before you reach port.
