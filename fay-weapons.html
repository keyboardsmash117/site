Fay Weapon Augments

Succor to the Sidhe quest system

Trade a Fay, Erlking, Dweomer, Ogre, or other eligible weapon to Callisto in Grauberg [S]. Fight Pixie NMs in Shadowreign zones. Receive an augmented version of your weapon.

  • 20 weapons — one for every job: Oberon's Rapier (RDM), Erlking's Sword (PLD), Rindomaru (SAM), Dweomer Maul (WHM), Fay Lance (DRG), and 15 more
  • 14 Shadowreign zones — each zone has its own augment pool with different stat possibilities (STR, DEX, Accuracy, Attack, Store TP, Double Attack, WSD, MAB, Cure Potency, etc.)
  • Random augments — up to 2 augments rolled per weapon from the zone's pool, with random values within defined ranges
  • 5 reroll attempts — don't like your augments? Refuse and try again, up to 5 times total
