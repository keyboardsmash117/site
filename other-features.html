Other Features

Additional custom content

Custom Content

  • Ferry Pirates — pirate crews board the ferry routes. Scaled mobs with linked aggro spawn on deck. Clear them before port.
  • Campaign Battles — custom Wings of the Goddess campaign system with Allied Notes rewards. Battles fire regularly across WotG zones.
  • Wedding Ceremony — full ceremony system. Two players exchange signed Matrimony Rings at Lovinia NPCs near Matrimonial Coffers, choose a venue, get teleported to the ceremony, and receive Wedding Rings. City fireworks display for 7 days after the ceremony.
  • Abyssea Boosted NMs — enhanced Abyssea Notorious Monsters with increased light drop rates (80%).
  • World First Announcements — server-wide announcements when a player achieves a first on the server.

Login Points

  • Permanent Login Campaign — earn 500 points on your first login each day, 100 on each subsequent login. Spend points at the Greeter Moogle on Trust Ciphers, mounts, rings, seals, and more. Full prize list →

Quality of Life

  • Homepoint Heal — heal to full HP/MP when touching a homepoint crystal.
  • Claim Shield — first to engage a mob has claim. Prevents griefing and stolen kills.
  • Auto Linkshell — new characters automatically receive a linkpearl for the server's linkshell on first login.
  • Conquest NPCs Always Up — conquest regional merchants are always available regardless of nation control.
  • Mission Skip NPC — talk to Lily in the hub zone to skip completed expansion mission lines.
  • Mission Item Shop — !shop and !shop 2 commands sell Rare/Ex items needed for mission progression at 1 gil each.
  • !buff Command — toggle leveling buffs. Levels 1–98: scaling Regen + Refresh + 250 TP + 200% EXP. Level 99: 200% Capacity Points bonus. Type !buff to toggle on or off.
  • Armor Commands — !af, !relic, and !empy open shops for your current main job's Artifact, Relic, and Empyrean armor sets (base versions, free).
  • Mog Wardrobe Unlocks — extra Mog Wardrobe slots unlock automatically as you complete expansion mission lines.
  • SOA Imprimatur Gate Removed — Seekers of Adoulin missions are accessible without fame or imprimatur requirements.
  • WotG Mission Decoupling — Wings of the Goddess story missions are not gated behind nation quests.
