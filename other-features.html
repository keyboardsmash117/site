More Features & QoL

Custom content, systems, and quality of life improvements

Custom Content

  • Hub Crafting Area — a dedicated crafting hub with four custom NPC types: an Advanced Support NPC that grants 15 minutes of any craft's Imagery buff (all 9 crafts: Fishing, Woodworking, Smithing, Goldsmithing, Clothcraft, Leathercraft, Bonecraft, Alchemy, Cooking), a Crystal Vendor stocking all 8 crystals and clusters, an Ephemeral Moogle for crystal storage (trade crystals or clusters to deposit), and 9 Guild Material Vendors — one per craft, always open, no guild rank required.
  • Campaign Battles — custom Wings of the Goddess campaign system. Beastmen armies attack WotG zones on a regular schedule. Defeat them to earn Allied Notes. Battles fire automatically across multiple zones.
  • Wedding Ceremony — full player wedding system. Two players exchange signed Matrimony Rings at Lovinia NPCs near Matrimonial Coffers, choose a venue, get teleported to the ceremony, and receive Wedding Rings. City fireworks display for 7 days after the ceremony.
  • Custom HNM System — enhanced Hyper Notorious Monster spawning with persistent time-of-death tracking across server restarts.

Server Events

  • Login Announcements — welcome messages when players log in, keeping the community informed of news and events.
  • Permanent Login Campaign — earn 500 points on your first login each day, 100 on each subsequent login. Spend points at the Greeter Moogle on Trust Ciphers, mounts, rings, seals, and more. Full prize list →
  • Trust Alter Ego Extravaganza — all seasonal trust events are permanently active. Summer, spring, autumn, and New Year trust NPCs are always available.

Progression & Systems

  • 4x EXP Rate — experience points earned from monsters are multiplied by 4. Level faster, spend more time on endgame content.
  • 2.5x Combat & Magic Skillup Rate — every weapon and magic skill levels faster, both in chance and amount per tick. Catching up on a new job is far less grindy.
  • Accelerated Craft Skillups — the craft chance multiplier is turned up dramatically. Skillups on any recipe within your range are effectively guaranteed, so progression through each craft is fast and consistent.
  • All Crafts to 110 — the common cap is raised to accommodate every craft maxing out. No specialization restriction — you can skill all 8 crafts to 110 on one character.
  • 50% Gathering Rates — harvesting, excavation, logging, and mining all return an item on 50% of attempts, up from retail's much lower base rates. Tool break chance remains 33%.
  • 85% Chocobo Digging during favorable weather, with the standard 100/day fatigue cap.
  • Equal Subjob Ratio — subjob level cap matches your main job (99/99 instead of retail's 99/49). Full access to all subjob abilities.
  • All Jobs Unlocked — all 22 jobs available at character creation. No need to do unlock quests.
  • Advanced Jobs From Level 1 — no 30+ main requirement, no flag quest, no "The Holy Crest" runaround. Pick them at creation and go.
  • All Maps Granted — every map is given at character creation and on every login. No need to buy or quest for maps.
  • Starting Outpost Warps — all nation outposts unlocked from the start, including Tu'Lia and Tavnazia.
  • Explorer Moogle — Explorer Moogle teleports unlock at level 10.
  • Magian Trials — enabled with streamlined kill counts. Faster trial completion than retail.
  • Blue Magic — fully implemented with 67+ learnable spells.
  • Fields of Valor / Grounds of Valor — training manuals enabled for structured leveling with bonus EXP pages.
  • Records of Eminence — daily and timed objectives for Sparks currency. Weekly exchange cap 100,000 sparks.
  • Gobbie Mystery Box — daily tally points for the mystery box system (45-day character age minimum).
  • Garrison — enabled with retail settings. One per Conquest Tally Week per player.
  • Abyssea Boosted NMs — selected Abyssea NMs are boosted with enhanced drop tables. 80% drop rate on pearl light.

Custom Commands

  • !buff — toggle leveling buffs. Levels 1–98: scaling Regen + Refresh + 250 TP + 200% EXP. Level 99: 200% Capacity Points bonus. Type !buff to toggle on or off.
  • !af / !relic / !empy — open shops for your current main job's Artifact, Relic, and Empyrean armor sets (base versions, free).
  • !shop / !shop 2 — buy Rare/Ex items needed for mission progression at 1 gil each. No more being stuck because you missed a key item.

Quality of Life

  • Homepoint Heal — heal to full HP/MP when touching a homepoint crystal. No need to rest or use items between fights.
  • Claim Shield — first to engage a mob has claim. Prevents griefing and stolen kills on open-world monsters.
  • Auto Linkshell — new characters automatically receive a linkpearl for the server's linkshell on first login. Instant community access.
  • Conquest NPCs Always Up — conquest regional merchants are always available regardless of which nation controls the region.
  • Mission Skip NPC — talk to Lily in the hub zone to skip completed expansion mission lines and jump ahead.
  • Mog Wardrobe Unlocks — extra Mog Wardrobe storage slots unlock automatically as you complete expansion mission lines.
  • SOA Imprimatur Gate Removed — Seekers of Adoulin missions are accessible without fame or imprimatur requirements.
  • WotG Mission Decoupling — Wings of the Goddess story missions are not gated behind Campaign nation quests.
  • Mazurka Generates Enmity — Bard's Mazurka movement speed song works properly with enmity generation.
  • Quest Workarounds — fixes for quests that have known issues in the upstream server code.
