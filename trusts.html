Trust Guide

Your NPC party members, explained

What Are Trusts?

Trusts are NPC companions you can summon to fight alongside you. They fill party roles just like real players — tanking, healing, dealing damage, and casting buffs. You can have up to 5 trusts active at once (filling a full party of 6 with you).

Each trust has a specific job and role. Some trusts have upgraded II versions that are stronger or behave differently. Building a good trust party is the key to soloing most content in FFXI.

All trusts listed here are available from the Login Points campaign — just log in daily and spend your points at the Greeter Moogle.

Quick Team-Building Tips

  • Every party needs a healer. Start with Karaha-Baruha (free at 100 pts) or save for Nashmeira II (the best).
  • A tank makes everything safer. August is the strongest tank trust in the game. Rughadjeen and Amchuchu are solid budget picks.
  • Support trusts multiply your damage. Koru-Moru (Haste + Refresh), Ovjang (Haste + Refresh), and Qultada (Corsair rolls) are top-tier.
  • Fill remaining slots with damage. Zeid II, Prishe II, and Gilgamesh are strong melee. Shantotto II and Ullegore nuke well.
  • II versions are upgrades. If you see a trust and their II version, the II is almost always better. Get both if you can.

Tank Tanks

Tanks hold the enemy's attention so your party doesn't get hit. They use abilities like Provoke, Flash, and high defense to keep hate.

August Tank

Paladin • Seekers of Adoulin

The best tank trust in the game. Extremely high defense, uses Flash, Holy II, Cure, and has massive HP. Your go-to tank for any content.

Rughadjeen Tank

Paladin • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

Solid Paladin tank with Cure, Holy, and Fast Cast. One of the Serpent Generals. Reliable for early-to-mid content.

Amchuchu Tank

Rune Fencer / Warrior • Seekers of Adoulin

Magic-focused tank using Rune Fencer. Vallation absorbs magic damage. Great against caster enemies where Paladin defense isn't enough.

Halver Tank

Paladin / Warrior • San d'Oria

Temple Knight of San d'Oria. Hybrid PLD/WAR tank with both defensive and offensive abilities. Decent all-rounder.

Rahal Tank

Paladin • San d'Oria

Royal Knight commander. Classic Paladin with Cure, Flash, and Holy. Straightforward tank that does the job.

Mnejing Tank

Puppetmaster (Automaton) • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

Nashmeira's automaton, configured as a PLD-style tank. Uses Provoke and Flash. Unique in that it's a mechanical puppet, not a person.

Healer Healers

Healers keep the party alive with Cure spells, status removal, and sometimes Raise to revive fallen members. You always want at least one.

Nashmeira II Healer

White Mage • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

The best healer trust. Afflatus Solace boosts her Cures, she prioritizes healing, and has Raise. Upgrade from base Nashmeira who was a melee hybrid.

Upgrade of Nashmeira (not in Login Points). The II version is a dedicated healer instead of a melee/healer hybrid.

Karaha-Baruha Healer

White Mage • Windurst

Windurst's greatest summoner in the story, but functions as a WHM trust. Cures, bar-element spells, and basic buffs. A solid starter healer available in Set 1.

Cid Healer

Scholar (Light Arts) • Bastok

The legendary engineer, here as a Scholar healer. Light Arts mode gives him Cure, Regen, and Raise. A good alternative healer with Scholar's versatility.

Adelheid Healer

Scholar • Seekers of Adoulin

Switches between Light Arts (healing) and Dark Arts (nuking). Flexible Scholar who can heal when needed and deal magic damage when the party is healthy.

Ferreous Coffin Healer

Paladin (Healer) • Chains of Promathia

Despite being a PLD, this trust functions primarily as a healer. Has Cure and Raise. An unusual pick but works in a pinch.

Uka Totlihn Healer

Dancer • Wings of the Goddess

Dancer healer who uses Waltzes (TP-based heals) instead of MP. Sambas give the party drain effects on melee hits. Great for sustained fights where MP runs low.

Mumor Healer

Dancer • Wings of the Goddess

Another Dancer healer/support. Uses Sambas and Waltzes to sustain the party. The famous Star Onion Brigade dancer from the Wings era.

Has an upgrade: Mumor II shifts to magic DD focus with magic burst damage.

Prishe II Healer

Monk • Chains of Promathia

Primarily a melee DD, but has emergency Cures. Not a main healer — think of her as a damage dealer who can spot-heal when things get rough.

Support Support & Buffers

Support trusts buff your party with Haste, Refresh, Protect, Shell, auras, and Corsair rolls. They multiply everyone else's effectiveness.

Koru-Moru Support

Red Mage • Windurst

Top-tier support. Casts Haste, Refresh, Protect, Shell, and Dispel on enemies. Red Mage buffs are the backbone of any party. One of the most recommended trusts.

Ovjang Support

Red Mage • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

Nashmeira's other automaton. Functions as a RDM support with Haste, Refresh, and nukes. Pairs perfectly with Nashmeira II for a healer+buffer combo.

King of Hearts Support

Red Mage / White Mage • Chains of Promathia

Cardian automaton from Windurst. Primary buffer with RDM spells. Haste, Refresh, and healing backup. Reliable all-around support.

Qultada Support

Corsair • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

Corsair who rolls party buffs (Phantom Roll). Rolls give percentage-based boosts to stats, attack, magic, and more. Unique buff source that stacks with everything.

Luzaf Support

Corsair / Ninja • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

Another Corsair with rolls, plus Quick Draw ranged attacks. More offense-oriented than Qultada. The legendary pirate king from the TOAU story.

Star Sibyl Support

Geomancer • Windurst

Leader of Windurst. Provides an Indi-Acumen aura (magic attack bonus) to nearby allies. Passive support — she doesn't fight, just boosts. Unkillable.

Sakura Support

Geomancer • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

Geomancer who provides a Regen aura to nearby allies. Passive healing that ticks constantly. Stack her with a real healer for extra safety.

A Moogle Support

Geomancer • General

Provides a Refresh aura (MP regen) to nearby allies. Essential for mage-heavy parties. Pair with Sakura for Regen + Refresh passive healing.

Rainemard Support

Red Mage • Wings of the Goddess

Red Mage who specializes in Enspell buffs (elemental damage on melee swings). Good for melee-heavy parties. Also provides standard RDM buffs.

Fablinix Support

Red Mage • Windurst

Goblin Red Mage with ranged attacks. Provides RDM support buffs. A quirky choice — functional but not top-tier compared to Koru-Moru.

Kupofried Support

Moogle • Rhapsodies of Vana'diel

The Moogle hero of Rhapsodies. Pure support with no combat ability. Provides passive benefits to the party. A lore pick more than a power pick.

Monberaux Support

Alchemist • Jeuno

The doctor from Upper Jeuno. Uses Mix abilities for unique support effects. An unusual trust with niche utility.

Melee DD Melee Damage Dealers

Melee DDs get in close and deal physical damage with weapons. They build TP through auto-attacks and spend it on weapon skills.

Zeid Melee DD

Dark Knight • Bastok / Zilart

The mysterious Galka swordsman. Uses Absorb/Drain spells and dark abilities like Last Resort and Souleater for big damage bursts.

Has an upgrade: Zeid II adds Stun priority and improved behavior.

Zeid II Melee DD

Dark Knight • Bastok / Zilart

Upgraded Zeid with Stun priority. Interrupts dangerous enemy abilities. Stronger overall damage and smarter ability usage than the base version.

Lion Melee DD

Thief-like • Zilart

The heroine of Rise of the Zilart. Uses Grapeshot (stun WS) and evasion. Agile melee with some ranged capability.

Has an upgrade: Lion II adds Utsusemi shadows for survivability.

Lion II Melee DD

Ranger-like • Zilart

Upgraded Lion with Utsusemi shadow images. Harder to kill and deals more consistent damage. A solid all-around melee choice.

Naja Salaheem Melee DD

Thief • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

The Salaheem's Sentinels president. Uses Focus, Dodge, and Counterstance for evasion-based melee with her signature weapon Peacebreaker.

Aldo Melee DD

Thief • Zilart / Jeuno

Leader of the Tenshodo. Sneak Attack and Bully for burst damage. A straightforward Thief damage dealer.

Maximilian Melee DD

Thief / Ninja • Seekers of Adoulin

Dual-wielding THF/NIN. High attack speed from dual wield. Good sustained damage from the constant stream of hits.

Gilgamesh Melee DD

Samurai • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

The emperor of Aht Urhgan himself. Samurai with Hasso and Meditate for fast TP gain and powerful weapon skills. A strong melee pick.

Mildaurion Melee DD

Samurai • Seekers of Adoulin

Adoulin Samurai who excels at opening skillchains. Pair with another DD who can close the chain for massive burst damage.

Morimar Melee DD

Samurai • Seekers of Adoulin

Samurai who closes skillchains instead of opening them. Pairs well with Mildaurion or any trust that opens chains. Strong finisher.

Tenzen II Melee DD

Samurai • Chains of Promathia

Upgraded Tenzen, the katana-wielding hero of Promathia. Ranged capability with Samurai skills. Better AI and damage than the base version.

Noillurie Melee DD

Samurai / Paladin • San d'Oria

Hybrid SAM/PLD who uses Hasso and Third Eye plus has some healing. An unusual mix that can off-tank in emergencies.

D. Shantotto Melee DD

Black Mage / Dark Knight • Windurst

The "dark" Shantotto. Wields a scythe and casts nukes — a true melee/magic hybrid. Darkness-element focused. Unpredictable but powerful.

Areuhat Melee DD

Dark Knight • San d'Oria

Aggressive DRK who uses Berserk, Aggressor, and Blood Rage for raw damage output. All offense, no defense. Hits hard.

Rongelouts Melee DD

Warrior • Seekers of Adoulin

Warrior with Berserk, Aggressor, and Warcry (party attack boost). Straightforward physical damage with a party buff.

Abquhbah Melee DD

Warrior • Seekers of Adoulin

Another Warrior with Berserk and Warcry. Similar to Rongelouts. Pick whichever you like — they fill the same role.

Lhe Lhangavo Melee DD

Monk • Seekers of Adoulin

Monk with Dodge, Chakra (self-heal), and Impetus (crit boost). Fast attacks with fists. Good sustained damage.

Lilisette II Melee DD

Dancer • Wings of the Goddess

The heroine of Wings of the Goddess, upgraded. Dancer who focuses on melee damage with Sambas and Waltzes for self-sustain.

Mayakov Melee DD

Dancer • Seekers of Adoulin

Offensive Dancer with Saber Dance and Feather Step. More damage-focused than the healer Dancers (Uka, Mumor).

Lehko Habhoka Melee DD

Ranger (hybrid) • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

Mithra pirate with double/triple attack. Melee/magic hybrid with Debonair Rush WS. Flashy and effective.

Lhu Mhakaracca Melee DD

Beastmaster / Warrior • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

Beastmaster who uses Feral Howl. A niche melee pick with some utility from BST abilities.

Ingrid II Melee DD

White Mage / Warrior • Wings of the Goddess

Upgraded Ingrid who shifted from healer to melee DD/nuker. Despite the WHM job, she prioritizes damage. An unexpected powerhouse.

Darrcuiln Melee DD

Beast Form • Seekers of Adoulin

Fights in beast form. Immune to most buffs but deals solid damage. A unique trust that doesn't play by normal rules.

Teodor Melee DD

Physical DD • Seekers of Adoulin

Physical melee with some magic burst capability. A hybrid damage dealer from the Adoulin storyline.

Abenzio Melee DD

Goobbue • Seekers of Adoulin

A Goobbue creature trust. High HP with counter attacks. Not a person — literally a plant monster fighting for you. Fun and tanky.

Babban Melee DD

Mandragora • Seekers of Adoulin

A Mandragora creature trust. Small but scrappy with counter attacks. Another creature trust like Abenzio.

Brygid Melee DD

NPC • Seekers of Adoulin

Minimal combat ability. More of a lore/collection trust than a combat pick. Use other DDs for serious content.

Rosulatia Melee DD

Plantoid Caster • Seekers of Adoulin

A plant creature that casts Earth-element magic. Unusual trust with niche appeal. Not a top damage pick.

Magic DD Magic Damage Dealers

Magic DDs cast offensive spells. They excel at magic bursting (casting right after a skillchain for bonus damage) and AoE situations.

Shantotto II Magic DD

Black Mage • Windurst

The upgraded version of FFXI's most iconic character. Pure BLM nuker with Haste self-buff and occasional melee. Powerful magic damage.

Ullegore Magic DD

Black Mage • Seekers of Adoulin

Dedicated BLM nuker with no melee. Pure spellcasting. Drops big nukes and focuses entirely on magic damage.

Kukki-Chebukki Magic DD

Black Mage • Windurst

One of the Chebukki triplets. BLM nuker. Pair with siblings Makki-Chebukki and another mage for a fun all-Tarutaru team.

Kayeel-Payeel Magic DD

Black Mage • Windurst

Another Tarutaru BLM. Straightforward nuker. Multiple BLM trusts let you build an all-mage party for magic burst chains.

Leonoyne Magic DD

Black Mage / Paladin • San d'Oria

Ice-element hybrid BLM/PLD. Unusual combination gives her some survivability along with her nuking. A tankier mage option.

Robel-Akbel Magic DD

Red Mage (Nuker) • Seekers of Adoulin

Unlike support RDMs, this one focuses on nuking and magic bursts. RDM damage spells with magic burst focus. Good magic DD pick.

Mumor II Magic DD

Dancer (Nuker) • Wings of the Goddess

Upgraded Mumor shifted from Dancer healer to magic burst damage dealer. A complete role change from the base version.

Base Mumor is a Dancer healer. Mumor II is a magic DD — totally different role.

Ranged DD Ranged Damage Dealers

Ranged DDs attack from a distance with bows, guns, or crossbows. They take less damage from AoE attacks and can keep dealing damage while staying safe.

Semih Lafihna Ranged DD

Ranger • Windurst

Pure ranged attacker with extremely high TP gain. Fires weapon skills frequently. One of the better ranged DD trusts.

Najelith Ranged DD

Ranger • Treasures of Aht Urhgan

Stays at range and fires arrows/bolts. A dedicated ranged attacker who avoids melee entirely. Safe and consistent damage.

Elivira Ranged DD

Ranger / Warrior • San d'Oria

RNG/WAR hybrid who can both melee and shoot. Flexible damage dealer who adapts to the situation.

Margret Ranged DD

Ranger / Thief • San d'Oria

RNG/THF hybrid with both melee and ranged capability. Can close skillchains from range. Versatile damage dealer.

Makki-Chebukki Ranged DD

Ranger / Black Mage • Windurst

The ranged Chebukki triplet. RNG/BLM hybrid who shoots and nukes. Pair with Kukki for a double Chebukki magic party.

Kuyin Hathdenna Ranged DD

Ranged Support • Wings of the Goddess

Ranged support NPC who doesn't auto-attack. More of a supportive presence than a damage dealer. Niche pick.
