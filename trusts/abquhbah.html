Abquhbah

Melee DD — Warrior

Who is Abquhbah?

Abquhbah is a fierce Galka warrior encountered during the Seekers of Adoulin storyline. He is a straightforward, no-nonsense fighter who charges headlong into battle. His brute-force approach makes him a reliable source of physical damage in any party.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD (damage dealer)
Main JobWarrior (WAR)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 5)

Key Abilities

  • Berserk — Temporarily boosts his attack power at the cost of reduced defense. This is a Warrior signature ability that makes every hit land harder.
  • Warcry — A party-wide attack boost that benefits everyone fighting alongside him. If you are a melee damage dealer, this buff helps you too.
  • Defender — Raises his defense at the cost of attack power. He uses this when things get dangerous, helping him survive longer in tough fights.
  • Weapon Skills — As a Warrior, Abquhbah uses powerful weapon skills when his TP gauge fills up. Weapon skills are special attacks that deal heavy burst damage.

When to Use Abquhbah

Abquhbah is a simple, effective damage dealer. Warriors are the most straightforward melee job in FFXI: they hit things hard and have buffs to hit even harder. He works well when you just need more raw physical damage in your party. Pair him with a tank like August and a healer, and he will reliably contribute damage without complications.
