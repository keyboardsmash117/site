Mihli Aliapoh

Healer — White Mage

Who is Mihli Aliapoh?

Mihli Aliapoh is a Mithra White Mage who serves as one of the Immortals in the Empire of Aht Urhgan. She is a skilled healer who supports the Empress's elite forces during the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline. Despite the dangers of the Near East, her dedication to her allies never wavers.

What They Do

RoleHealer
Main JobWhite Mage (WHM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
How to ObtainStory mission (Treasures of Aht Urhgan)

Key Abilities

  • Cure spells — heals party members with strong, responsive healing
  • Protect / Shell — buffs the party with damage reduction magic
  • Haste — speeds up party members' attack rate, boosting overall damage output
  • Status removal — removes debuffs with -na spells to keep the party fighting

When to Use Mihli Aliapoh

Mihli Aliapoh is a capable White Mage trust unlocked through the Treasures of Aht Urhgan story. She provides solid healing and party support including Haste, making her a step up from the basic nation healer trusts. A great choice for mid-game content when you need reliable healing with some extra buffing utility.
