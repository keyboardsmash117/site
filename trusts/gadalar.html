Gadalar

Nuker — Black Mage

Who is Gadalar?

Gadalar is a Mamool Ja black mage who plays a role in the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline. As a member of the lizard-like Mamool Ja beastmen, he is an unusual ally who wields powerful destructive magic against your shared enemies.

What They Do

RoleNuker
Main JobBlack Mage (BLM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
How to ObtainComplete TOAU missions

Key Abilities

  • Tier IV Nukes — Casts high-tier elemental magic for heavy damage
  • Ancient Magic — Uses powerful ancient magic spells for burst damage
  • Elemental Debuffs — Applies elemental enfeebles to weaken enemies
  • Manafont — Eliminates MP cost for a short duration, enabling spell spam

Ability Summary

  • Spells: None family
  • Tier-scaling stat: Magic Attack, Magic Accuracy (1.0× → 2.5×)

When to Use Gadalar

Gadalar fills the magic damage role in your party. He is best used when you need raw magical burst damage, especially against enemies with high physical defense. Make sure you have a solid tank to keep hate off him, as nukers generate significant enmity.
