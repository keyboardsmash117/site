Ayame (UC)

Melee Damage Dealer — Samurai

Who is Ayame (UC)?

Ayame is a Hume Samurai from Bastok and one of the Mythril Musketeers. Her Unity Concord version features faster TP gain and more effective weapon skill usage, making her a potent melee damage dealer. Unity Concord trusts are enhanced versions obtained through the Unity faction system with improved combat AI.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer

Sub JobWarrior (WAR)

How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Meditate — instantly gains TP for faster weapon skill use
  • Hasso — increases attack speed and strength in offensive stance
  • Third Eye — anticipates and avoids an incoming attack
  • Sekkanoki — next weapon skill requires less TP
  • Enhanced TP/WS cycle — faster TP gain and better weapon skill timing compared to base Ayame

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Hasso, Meditate, Third Eye

When to Use Ayame (UC)

Ayame (UC) is one of the stronger melee damage trusts thanks to Samurai's naturally fast TP generation. Her Meditate ability means she uses weapon skills frequently, and the UC upgrade makes her even more efficient. She pairs well with skillchain-capable trusts and is a great choice when you need reliable physical damage output.
