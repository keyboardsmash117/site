Matsui-P

Special / Novelty — NPC

Who is Matsui-P?

Matsui-P is a special promotional trust based on Akihiko Matsui, the longtime producer and director of Final Fantasy XI. This novelty trust was introduced as a fun tribute to the man who has guided FFXI for over two decades. He is more of a collector's item and joke trust than a serious combat ally.

When to Use Matsui-P

Matsui-P is a novelty trust for fun and collection purposes. He is not meant for serious combat situations and should not be relied on as a primary party member. Summon him when you want a laugh or to show off a rare trust to friends. For actual content, use one of the many combat-capable trusts instead.
