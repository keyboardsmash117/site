Areuhat

Melee DD — Dark Knight

Who is Areuhat?

Areuhat is a Dark Knight from San d'Oria who has fully embraced the philosophy of pure offense. Where other Dark Knights balance their aggression with some defensive magic or utility, Areuhat throws caution to the wind and stacks every offensive buff available. He is the definition of a glass cannon — maximum damage, minimum survivability.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD (Pure Offense)

Sub JobNone

Key Abilities

  • Berserk — a Warrior ability that greatly increases attack power but lowers defense. Combined with Last Resort (which also trades defense for attack), Areuhat stacks two major offensive buffs at once, making him extremely fragile but devastating.
  • Aggressor — boosts accuracy at the cost of evasion. This ensures Areuhat's attacks actually land, which matters because missed attacks waste all that offensive power.
  • Blood Rage — further boosts critical hit rate, adding even more burst damage potential on top of his already-high attack power.

When to Use Areuhat

Areuhat is for players who want raw damage and have the healing to support it. He stacks Berserk, Last Resort, Aggressor, and Blood Rage — all of which boost his offense while shredding his defense. This means he hits like a truck but goes down fast if enemies turn on him. Make sure you have a good healer trust (like Apururu or Yoran-Oran) and ideally a tank trust to keep enemies off him. When properly supported, Areuhat is one of the hardest-hitting melee trusts available. When unsupported, he is a liability.
