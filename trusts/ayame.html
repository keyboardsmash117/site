Ayame

Melee DD — Samurai

Who is Ayame?

Ayame is a Hume Samurai from Bastok and the sister of Nolan, a fellow Musketeer. Trained in the art of the katana, she is a skilled warrior who features prominently in the Bastok mission storyline. Her determination and combat prowess make her one of the Republic's most capable fighters.

What They Do

RoleMelee damage dealer
Main JobSamurai (SAM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionBastok (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (Bastok)

Key Abilities

  • Meditate — rapidly builds TP for more frequent weapon skills
  • Great Katana weapon skills — delivers strong multi-hit weapon skills for burst damage
  • Third Eye — anticipates and dodges incoming attacks for survivability

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Hasso, Third Eye, Meditate

When to Use Ayame

Ayame is an excellent melee DD trust thanks to Samurai's natural TP generation from Meditate, meaning she uses weapon skills frequently. She works well in any party composition that already has a tank and healer covered. A Unity Concord (UC) version of Ayame is also available for players in her Unity group.
