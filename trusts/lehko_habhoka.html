Lehko Habhoka

Melee DD — Ranger Hybrid

Who is Lehko Habhoka?

Lehko Habhoka is a charismatic Mithra pirate from the Treasures of Aht Urhgan expansion. He is a swashbuckling rogue who fights with a blend of melee strikes and ranged attacks. His unpredictable fighting style and roguish charm make him a fan favorite among FFXI players.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD (hybrid melee/ranged)
Main JobRanger hybrid
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 1)

Key Abilities

  • Double Attack — A trait that gives each melee swing a chance to strike twice. This effectively doubles his damage on lucky hits and helps him build TP faster.
  • Triple Attack — Even better than Double Attack, this gives a chance to land three hits in a single swing. When it triggers, TP and damage skyrocket.
  • Ranged Attacks — Lehko can switch to ranged combat, attacking from a distance. This keeps him safer against enemies with dangerous close-range abilities.
  • Weapon Skills — He uses weapon skills from both melee and ranged categories, giving him flexibility in how he deals damage.

Ability Summary

  • Spells: None family
  • Passive: Magic Accuracy: power, +15 Double Attack, +10 Triple Attack

When to Use Lehko Habhoka

Lehko Habhoka is a versatile damage dealer who can fight up close or at range. His Double and Triple Attack traits mean he generates TP quickly and hits frequently. He is a good general-purpose damage Trust that works in most situations. Available in Set 1, he is one of the earliest Trusts you can pick up, making him a solid choice for newer players building their first Trust collection.
