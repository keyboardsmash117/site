Kupofried

Support — Moogle

Who is Kupofried?

Kupofried is a heroic Moogle who plays a central role in the Rhapsodies of Vana'diel storyline, the final grand expansion of FFXI that ties together all the previous stories. He guides the player through their journey across time and space to save Vana'diel from destruction. He is brave, selfless, and one of the most memorable Moogles in the game.

What They Do

RoleSupport (Passive)
Main JobMoogle
Sub JobNone
ExpansionRhapsodies of Vana'diel
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 5)

Key Abilities

  • Passive Party Benefits — Kupofried provides background support effects to the party. He does not cast spells, does not fight, and does not heal. His contribution is entirely passive, providing subtle bonuses just by being present in your party.
  • No Combat — Like the Star Sibyl, Kupofried does not engage in combat. He will not attack enemies or cast offensive spells. He takes up a trust slot purely for his passive benefits.

When to Use Kupofried

Kupofried is primarily a lore and collection pick. If you love the Rhapsodies of Vana'diel story and want your favorite Moogle companion adventuring with you, he is a great choice for that emotional connection. For pure optimization, other support trusts like Koru-Moru, Qultada, or the Geomancer trusts provide more impactful and visible benefits. Think of Kupofried as a trust you bring because you like him, not because you need him.
