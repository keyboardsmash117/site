Tenzen

Melee DD — Samurai

Who is Tenzen?

Tenzen is a Hume Samurai from the Far East who carries the Phoenix within him, making him a figure of immense spiritual importance. He travels to the Middle Lands during the Chains of Promathia storyline to combat the threat of the Emptiness, becoming one of the player's most steadfast allies in the fight against Promathia.

What They Do

RoleMelee damage dealer
Main JobSamurai (SAM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionChains of Promathia
How to ObtainStory mission (Chains of Promathia)

Key Abilities

  • Meditate — rapidly builds TP for frequent weapon skill usage
  • Great Katana weapon skills — powerful multi-hit attacks for strong burst damage
  • Third Eye — anticipates and dodges incoming physical attacks

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Hasso, Third Eye, Hagakure, Meditate

When to Use Tenzen

Tenzen is a strong Samurai DD trust who generates TP quickly and uses weapon skills frequently. He is an excellent melee damage dealer for mid-game content once you have progressed through Chains of Promathia. An upgraded version, Tenzen II, is available from Login Points and provides even stronger performance at endgame.
