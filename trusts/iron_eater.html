Iron Eater

Melee DD — Warrior

Who is Iron Eater?

Iron Eater is a Galka Warrior and a proud member of the Mythril Musketeers, Bastok's elite military unit. He is a towering figure of immense strength who features in the Bastok mission storyline. Like many Galka, he carries a deep sense of duty and fights to protect the Republic he calls home.

What They Do

RoleMelee damage dealer
Main JobWarrior (WAR)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionBastok (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (Bastok)

Key Abilities

  • Great Axe weapon skills — delivers heavy-hitting weapon skills for strong burst damage
  • Berserk — increases attack power at the cost of defense for maximum damage
  • Defender — boosts defense when survivability is needed over raw damage

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Provoke, Berserk, Restraint

When to Use Iron Eater

Iron Eater is a reliable melee DD trust available to Bastok players from the Trust quest. His great axe attacks hit hard and he provides steady damage output. He is a good complement to other Bastok trusts like Volker and Ayame, and works well in any party that already has tanking and healing covered.
