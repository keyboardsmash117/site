Lion II

Melee DD — Thief with Ninjutsu shadows

Who is Lion II?

Lion II is the upgraded version of Lion, the Mithran tracker from Rise of the Zilart. She keeps the same Thief/marksmanship combat style as base Lion, but layers ninjutsu shadow magic on top — a Thief/Ninja hybrid that's far harder to kill than the base version.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD with defensive self-sustain
Main JobThief (THF) with Ninjutsu magic
Sub JobNone (uses NIN magic natively)
ExpansionRise of the Zilart
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 5)

Key Abilities

  • Utsusemi (highest priority) — ninjutsu magic that creates shadow copies. Each shadow absorbs one physical attack completely, zero damage taken on that hit. Lion II recasts it the moment her Copy Image effect drops — this is her defining upgrade over base Lion.
  • Grapeshot — same reactive interrupt as base Lion. Fires when an enemy readies a weapon skill, TP move, or begins casting a spell. Stuns the target and cancels the action.
  • Powder Keg — her signature weapon skill. Triggers a teamwork dialogue when used alongside Zeid, Prishe II, Lilisette II, or Arciela II.
  • Aggressive TP usage — dumps TP at 3000 with randomised skill choice for consistent damage uptime.

When to Use Lion II

Lion II is a clear upgrade over base Lion in almost every situation — she keeps all of Lion's reactive Grapeshot interrupts and adds Utsusemi shadows for physical mitigation. The shadow layer dramatically reduces the healing burden on your party, especially against single-target physical enemies.

Note she is still a Thief, not a Ranger — she uses Marksmanship weapon skills (Grapeshot / Powder Keg) but her core combat behaviour is Thief-style aggressive melee with shadow backup.
