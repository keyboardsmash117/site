Kukki-Chebukki

Magic DD — Black Mage

Who is Kukki-Chebukki?

Kukki-Chebukki is one of the three Chebukki triplets, a trio of mischievous Tarutaru siblings from Windurst. Each triplet specializes in a different combat style. Kukki is the magic-focused sibling, taking up the Black Mage mantle to blast enemies with elemental spells. The triplets are beloved for their playful personalities.

What They Do

RoleMagic DD (nuker)
Main JobBlack Mage (BLM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWindurst
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 4)

Key Abilities

  • Elemental Nukes — Kukki casts destructive elemental spells like any Black Mage. Fire, Blizzard, Thunder, and their higher tiers are her tools of the trade.
  • AoE Spells — She can cast area-of-effect spells that damage multiple enemies at once, useful when fighting groups of monsters.
  • Magic Burst — She follows up skillchains with matching elemental spells for boosted damage, just like other Black Mage Trusts.
  • Chebukki Synergy — Summoning all three Chebukki triplets (Kukki, Makki, and Cherukiki) together is a fun thematic choice, though not necessarily the most powerful party composition.

Ability Summary

  • Spells: None family
  • Passive: Magic Attack: power, Magic Accuracy: power

When to Use Kukki-Chebukki

Kukki-Chebukki is a serviceable Black Mage Trust. She is not as powerful as Shantotto II, but she gets the job done as a nuker. She is a good choice if you want to summon the full Chebukki triplet team for fun, or if you need a BLM from Set 4 specifically. For raw magical damage output, Shantotto II is the stronger pick, but Kukki is a perfectly fine alternative.
