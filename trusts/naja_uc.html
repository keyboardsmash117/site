Naja Salaheem (UC)

Melee Damage Dealer — Thief

Who is Naja Salaheem (UC)?

Naja Salaheem is the president of Salaheem's Sentinels, the mercenary company you join in the Treasures of Aht Urhgan expansion. She is a fierce Mithra who runs her business with an iron fist. Her Unity Concord version boosts her evasion and damage output with enhanced Thief AI.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobThief (THF)
Sub JobWarrior (WAR)
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Sneak Attack — bonus damage from behind the target
  • Trick Attack — enmity transfer and bonus damage
  • High Evasion — naturally dodges many incoming attacks
  • Assassin's Charge — critical hit rate boost
  • Improved Damage AI — UC version is better at chaining Sneak/Trick Attack with weapon skills

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Focus, Dodge, Counterstance

When to Use Naja Salaheem (UC)

Naja (UC) is an evasive melee damage dealer who can dodge attacks while dealing solid damage. Her improved evasion means she takes less damage than other melee trusts, reducing healer strain. She works well as a damage dealer who can survive in the fray even without constant healing. A fun pick for fans of the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline.
