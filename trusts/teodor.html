Teodor

Melee DD — Physical / Magic Hybrid

Who is Teodor?

Teodor is a scholarly warrior from the Seekers of Adoulin expansion who combines physical combat with magical knowledge. He fights in melee but also weaves in magic bursts, making him a true hybrid damage dealer who can contribute in multiple ways.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD (physical/magic hybrid)
Main JobPhysical DD (unique)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 2)

Key Abilities

  • Melee Attacks — Teodor fights up close with physical strikes, building TP and using weapon skills like any standard melee fighter.
  • Magic Burst — After a skillchain (when weapon skills combine in sequence for bonus damage), Teodor can follow up with a magic burst. A magic burst is a spell timed to match the skillchain's element, dealing massively increased magic damage.
  • Hybrid Damage — By combining physical weapon skills with magical follow-ups, Teodor deals both types of damage. This is useful against enemies that resist one type but not the other.
  • Skillchain Participation — He actively tries to create or extend skillchains, which are powerful combo attacks formed when weapon skills are used in the right order.

When to Use Teodor

Teodor is interesting for players who want a Trust that does more than just auto-attack and weapon skill. His magic burst capability adds a layer of damage that pure melee Trusts cannot match when skillchains are happening. He works best in parties where skillchains are frequent, letting him capitalize with big magic burst damage. For general use, a pure melee DD may be simpler, but Teodor rewards parties that take advantage of the skillchain system.
