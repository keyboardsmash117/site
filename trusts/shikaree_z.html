Shikaree Z

Ranged DD — Ranger

Who is Shikaree Z?

Shikaree Z is a Mithra bounty hunter encountered during the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline. She is one of three Shikaree sisters who work as mercenaries in the Near East, relying on her sharpshooting skills to take down targets from a distance.

What They Do

RoleRanged Damage Dealer
Main JobRanger (RNG)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
How to ObtainComplete TOAU missions

Key Abilities

  • Ranged Attacks — Deals consistent damage from range using a bow
  • Barrage — Fires multiple arrows in a single attack round for burst damage
  • Shadowbind — Roots enemies in place temporarily
  • Sharpshot — Increases ranged accuracy for a period of time

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Ancient Circle, Jump, High Jump, Super Jump
  • Spells: Cure, Erase, Haste, Poisona, Paralyna, Blindna, Silena, Stona, Viruna, Cursna, Cure family
  • Passive: +164 Jump TP Bonus
  • Tier-scaling stat: Store TP (1.0× → 2.5×)

When to Use Shikaree Z

Shikaree Z is a solid pick when you want ranged damage that stays safely out of melee range. She works well in parties where you already have a tank and healer and just need more damage output. Her Barrage ability provides good burst damage windows.
