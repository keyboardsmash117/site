Karaha-Baruha

Starter Healer — White Mage

Who is Karaha-Baruha?

Karaha-Baruha is one of the most important figures in Windurst's history -- he was the greatest summoner the Federation of Windurst ever produced. In the game's lore, he sacrificed himself to protect Windurst during the Crystal War. Despite being famous as a summoner, his trust version functions as a White Mage, focusing on healing magic rather than summoning avatars.

Key Abilities

  • Cure spells — Restores HP (health) to injured party members. This is the core of any healer. He will automatically Cure whoever in the party is taking damage.
  • Bar-element spells (Barfire, Barblizzard, etc.) — These spells reduce the damage your party takes from a specific element. For example, Barfire makes everyone take less damage from fire attacks. Very helpful against enemies that use elemental magic.
  • Protect — A buff (beneficial spell) that reduces physical damage taken. Think of it as a magical armor boost.
  • Shell — A buff that reduces magic damage taken. The magical equivalent of Protect but for spells instead of physical hits.

When to Use Karaha-Baruha

Karaha-Baruha is your first healer. He is available in Set 1 from the Login Points vendor, making him one of the cheapest trusts to acquire. For new players working through early content, he does the job perfectly well -- he keeps the party alive with Cure spells and provides basic defensive buffs.

His Bar-element spells are a nice bonus that other early healers do not offer. If you are fighting enemies that use a lot of elemental magic (like bomb-type monsters that spam fire), he can noticeably reduce the damage your party takes.

Once you have enough Login Points to purchase Set 5, you should upgrade to Nashmeira II for harder content. Nashmeira II has stronger heals, Raise (to revive you if you fall), and Afflatus Solace. Karaha-Baruha is a solid stepping stone, but he lacks Raise and his healing output falls behind at higher levels.
