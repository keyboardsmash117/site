Jakoh Wahcondalo (UC)

Melee Damage Dealer — Monk

Who is Jakoh Wahcondalo (UC)?

Jakoh Wahcondalo is a Mithra Monk from Windurst who fights with her fists. This Unity Concord version gives her enhanced combat AI and improved stats, making her a more effective melee damage dealer. Unity Concord trusts are upgraded versions of existing trusts obtained through the Unity faction system.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobMonk (MNK)
Sub JobWarrior (WAR)
ExpansionWindurst
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Hundred Fists — dramatically increases attack speed for a burst of damage
  • Boost — enhances next attack's damage
  • Focus / Dodge — increases accuracy and evasion
  • Chakra — self-healing to stay in the fight longer
  • Combo / Asuran Fists — hand-to-hand weapon skills for strong burst damage

When to Use Jakoh Wahcondalo (UC)

Jakoh is a reliable melee damage dealer who can sustain herself with Chakra. Her Monk job gives her natural tankiness through HP and Counterstance. She works best as a secondary damage dealer alongside a proper tank. The UC upgrade makes her noticeably better at timing weapon skills and using Hundred Fists at opportune moments.
