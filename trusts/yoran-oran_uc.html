Yoran-Oran (UC)

Healer / Nuker — White Mage / Black Mage

Who is Yoran-Oran (UC)?

Yoran-Oran is a Tarutaru scholar from Windurst who dabbles in both white and black magic. His Unity Concord version enhances his dual-role capabilities, making him a flexible caster who can both heal and deal magic damage. Unity Concord trusts are upgraded versions obtained through the Unity faction system with improved AI.

What They Do

RoleHealer / Magic Damage Dealer
Main JobWhite Mage (WHM)
Sub JobBlack Mage (BLM)
ExpansionWindurst
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Cure spells — heals the party when HP drops
  • Elemental nukes — deals magic damage during downtime between heals
  • Protect / Shell — defensive party buffs
  • Raise — can revive fallen party members
  • Dual-role AI — switches between healing and nuking based on party needs

When to Use Yoran-Oran (UC)

Yoran-Oran (UC) is a jack-of-all-trades caster. He can fill a healer slot while contributing magic damage when the party is healthy. This makes him useful when you want healing coverage but do not want to dedicate a full slot to a pure healer. However, for harder content where dedicated healing is critical, a specialist like Apururu (UC) is safer.
