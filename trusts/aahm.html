Ark Angel HM

Tank / Melee Damage — Paladin / Warrior

Who is Ark Angel HM?

Ark Angel HM is the Hume Ark Angel from the Rise of the Zilart expansion. The Ark Angels are five powerful celestial warriors created by the Crystal Warriors to guard the Gates of Paradise. In FFXI's story, they are fearsome bosses that players must defeat to progress through Zilart missions. As a trust, AAHM fights alongside you as a Paladin/Warrior hybrid — tanking while dealing solid melee damage.

What They Do

RoleTank / Melee Damage Dealer
Main JobPaladin (PLD)
Sub JobWarrior (WAR)
ExpansionRise of the Zilart
How to ObtainSpecial unlock through Zilart storyline content

Key Abilities

  • Shield Bash — stuns enemies with shield strikes
  • Flash — generates enmity and blinds the target
  • Sentinel — reduces incoming damage while tanking
  • Provoke — from Warrior sub, forces enemy attention
  • Offensive tanking — combines PLD survivability with WAR aggression for a hybrid playstyle

When to Use Ark Angel HM

AAHM is a hybrid tank who can hold hate while contributing meaningful damage thanks to his Warrior sub job. He is a good choice when you want a tank who does more than just stand there. The Ark Angel trusts are iconic and powerful — if you have access to them, they are worth trying. AAHM works well as your party's main tank for general and mid-tier content.
