Kupipi

Healer — White Mage

Who is Kupipi?

Kupipi is a Tarutaru White Mage from Windurst who serves at the Optistery. She is one of the twin sisters (along with Cherukiki) and is known for her gentle, caring personality. As one of the first healers available through the Trust system, she has kept countless adventurers alive during their early journeys.

What They Do

RoleHealer
Main JobWhite Mage (WHM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWindurst (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (Windurst)

Key Abilities

  • Cure spells — heals party members to keep everyone alive
  • Protect / Shell — buffs the party with defensive magic to reduce incoming damage
  • Status removal — casts Paralyna, Poisona, and other removal spells to cleanse debuffs

When to Use Kupipi

Kupipi is one of the first healers you will have access to, making her essential for early leveling and mission content. She keeps the party healthy with Cure spells and provides basic buffing. As you unlock more advanced healer trusts, you may swap her out, but she is a dependable choice whenever you need a straightforward White Mage.
