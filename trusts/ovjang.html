Ovjang

Support — Red Mage

Who is Ovjang?

Ovjang is one of two automaton puppets that serve Empress Nashmeira of the Empire of Aht Urhgan. Along with his counterpart Mnejing, Ovjang accompanies Nashmeira throughout the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline. Despite being a mechanical puppet, he has a distinct personality and is fiercely loyal to the Empress.

What They Do

RoleSupport / Buffer
Main JobRed Mage (RDM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 3)

Key Abilities

  • Haste (melee targets) + Refresh (casters) — automatic party-wide buff cycling.
  • Slow, Paralyze, Silence — debuff rotation on the target.
  • Dispel — strips enemy buffs on sight.
  • Nuke rotation — cycles through all elemental nukes when skillchain windows are not available, so she contributes damage between buffs. Hybrid support/magic-DD.

Tier Upgrades

Ovjang's upgrades sharpen the nuke side of her kit — Fast Cast scaling plus Addle enfeeble plus Magic Attack bonus.

TierUnlocks
BaseHaste / Refresh / Slow / Paralyze / Silence / Dispel / nuke rotation.
Tier I+15 Fast Cast.
Tier II+30 Fast Cast total, Addle enfeeble added (reduces enemy magic damage + casting speed).
Tier III (MAX)+45 Fast Cast total, +30 Magic Attack — serious nuke-damage uplift on top of the support.

When to Use Ovjang

Ovjang is the damage-dealing support. She works excellently as part of the Puppetmaster automaton trio with Mnejing (tank) and Nashmeira II (healer) thanks to shared teamwork dialogue, but she stands on her own as a Koru-Moru alternative when you want buffs + enfeebles + real nuke pressure. At Tier III her Magic Attack boost puts her damage in the same tier as dedicated nukers.
