Ingrid II

Melee DD / Nuker — White Mage

Who is Ingrid II?

Ingrid is a mysterious figure from the Wings of the Goddess expansion, deeply tied to the Crystal War storyline. This upgraded version of Ingrid is deceptively classified as a White Mage. Despite her WHM job, she fights primarily as a melee attacker and offensive spell caster, making her one of the more unusual Trusts in the game.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD / Nuker (hybrid)
Main JobWhite Mage (WHM)
Sub JobWarrior (WAR)
ExpansionWings of the Goddess
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 5)

Key Abilities

  • Melee Combat — Despite being a White Mage, Ingrid II fights in melee range with her Warrior sub job providing offensive buffs. Do not expect her to be your healer.
  • Offensive Magic — She casts offensive spells including Holy and Banish, dealing light-element damage. These spells are especially effective against undead enemies.
  • Some Healing — She can cast Cure spells, but she prioritizes attacking over healing. Think of any healing she does as a bonus, not her main purpose.
  • Berserk — From her WAR sub job, she can boost her attack power, further cementing her role as a damage dealer rather than a support character.

When to Use Ingrid II

Do not summon Ingrid II expecting a healer. She is a damage dealer who happens to have the WHM job. Use her when you want a hybrid that can fight in melee and throw out offensive spells. She works best when you already have a dedicated healer in your party and just want another source of damage. Her light-element spells make her particularly useful against undead enemies.
