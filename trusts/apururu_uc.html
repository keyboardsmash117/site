Apururu (UC)

Healer — White Mage

Who is Apururu (UC)?

Apururu is a Tarutaru White Mage from Windurst and one of the most beloved healers in the trust system. Her Unity Concord version is a popular endgame healing pick — improved AI, faster cure reactions, and stronger abilities compared to base Apururu. Unity Concord trusts are enhanced variants obtained through the retail Unity system.

What They Do

RoleHealer
Main JobWhite Mage (WHM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWindurst
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Cure IV / Cure V — powerful single-target healing with excellent cure potency
  • Curaga — area-of-effect healing for the whole party
  • Raise — revives fallen party members, a critical ability many healer trusts lack
  • Haste / Protect / Shell — essential party buffs
  • Healing Priority AI — prioritizes keeping the party alive over everything else, reacting quickly to damage

When to Use Apururu (UC)

Apururu (UC) should be your go-to healer for almost any content. Her exceptional cure potency, access to Raise, and healing-focused AI make her one of the most reliable trusts in the game. She is especially valuable for harder content where party members take heavy damage. If you can only pick one healer trust, Apururu (UC) is one of the safest choices available.
