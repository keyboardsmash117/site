Apururu (UC)

Healer — White Mage

Who is Apururu (UC)?

Apururu is a Tarutaru White Mage from Windurst and one of the most beloved healers in the trust system. Her Unity Concord version is a popular endgame healing pick — improved AI, faster cure reactions, and stronger abilities compared to base Apururu. Unity Concord trusts are enhanced variants obtained through the retail Unity system.

What They Do

RoleHealer
Main JobWhite Mage (WHM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWindurst
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Cure IV / Cure V — powerful single-target healing with excellent cure potency
  • Curaga — area-of-effect healing for the whole party
  • Raise — revives fallen party members, a critical ability many healer trusts lack
  • Haste / Protect / Shell — essential party buffs
  • Healing Priority AI — prioritizes keeping the party alive over everything else, reacting quickly to damage

Tier Upgrades

Apururu (UC) is a tier-upgradeable trust. Each rank unlocks more Regen tiers, more cure potency, and eventually Raise.

TierUnlocks
BaseCure (up to V), Curaga (reactive to Sleep), full -na suite, Erase, Protectra, Shellra, Haste (melee target), Stoneskin self-buff, auto-Convert at MP < 25%.
Tier I+15 Cure Potency, Regen I & II spells added (auto-cast on party below 90% HP).
Tier II+15 more Cure Potency (30 total), Regen III and Haste II spells added.
Tier III (MAX)+20 more Cure Potency (50 total), Regen IV and Raise spells added (auto-Raise on KO'd party members).

When to Use Apururu (UC)

Apururu (UC) is a reliable healer at any tier. Her healing-focused AI prioritises cure cycling over damage, and her auto-Convert keeps her MP pool full on longer fights. At Tier III she becomes a full endgame healer (Raise + Regen IV + 50 Cure Potency).

She pairs well with any composition. Wear the Apururu Unity Shirt when summoning her to unlock a special teamwork dialogue.
