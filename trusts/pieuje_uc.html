Pieuje (UC)

Tank — Paladin

Who is Pieuje (UC)?

Prince Pieuje is a noble Elvaan from San d'Oria and a devout Temple Knight. This is his Unity Concord version — an enhanced variant obtained through the Unity Concord system, which offers upgraded versions of popular trusts with improved AI, better stats, and stronger abilities compared to their base counterparts.

Key Abilities

  • Flash — generates enmity to hold enemy attention
  • Cure spells — self-healing to stay alive while tanking
  • Shield Bash — stuns enemies briefly
  • Sentinel / Rampart — damage reduction cooldowns for surviving heavy hits
  • Enhanced AI — better at holding hate and using abilities at the right time compared to base Pieuje

When to Use Pieuje (UC)

Pieuje (UC) is a solid Paladin tank for general content. His Unity Concord upgrade gives him better tanking AI and improved stats over the base version. Pair him with a strong healer like Apururu (UC) and a damage dealer for a balanced party. He works well for leveling and mid-tier content where you need a reliable shield in front.
