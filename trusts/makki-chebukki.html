Makki-Chebukki

Ranged DD — Ranger / Black Mage Hybrid

Who is Makki-Chebukki?

Makki-Chebukki is one of the three Chebukki triplets from Windurst. While sibling Kukki-Chebukki focuses on pure magic and Cherukiki on healing, Makki takes the ranged combat path with a magical twist. His BLM sub job lets him mix ranged attacks with elemental spells, making him the most versatile of the three siblings.

What They Do

RoleRanged DD (ranged/magic hybrid)
Main JobRanger (RNG)
Sub JobBlack Mage (BLM)
ExpansionWindurst
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 5)

Key Abilities

  • Ranged Attacks — Makki fires at enemies from a distance, using bows for consistent physical ranged damage.
  • Elemental Nukes — From his BLM sub job, he can cast offensive elemental spells between ranged shots. This gives him both physical and magical damage options.
  • Hybrid Damage — Enemies that resist physical damage might be weak to magic, and vice versa. Makki can hit them with whichever type they are less resistant to.
  • Chebukki Triplet — Summoning all three Chebukki siblings (Makki, Kukki, and Cherukiki) together gives you ranged damage, magical damage, and healing all from one family.

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Barrage, Sharpshot, Flashy Shot
  • Passive: +100% max MP

When to Use Makki-Chebukki

Makki-Chebukki is a fun hybrid Trust for players who want both ranged and magical damage from one party slot. His dual damage types give him flexibility against different enemy resistances. He is also the obvious choice if you are running the full Chebukki triplet party for thematic fun. For pure ranged damage, a dedicated Ranger will outperform him, and for pure magic, a BLM is better. But for versatility in one slot, Makki delivers.
