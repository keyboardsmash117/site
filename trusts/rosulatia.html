Rosulatia

Ranged DD — Plantoid Caster

Who is Rosulatia?

Rosulatia is a plantoid creature from the Seekers of Adoulin expansion. She is a plant-based being who uses earth-element magic to attack from range. Like other creature-type Trusts such as Abenzio and Babban, she is not a humanoid character but rather a sentient plant that fights alongside adventurers.

What They Do

RoleRanged DD (magic caster)
Main JobPlantoid caster (unique creature)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 5)

Key Abilities

  • Earth-Element Magic — Rosulatia specializes in earth-element attacks, casting Stone spells and other earth-based magic. Earth magic is effective against lightning-element enemies.
  • Ranged Casting — She stays at range and casts spells rather than engaging in melee. This keeps her relatively safe from physical attacks.
  • Creature Type — As a non-humanoid creature, she may not benefit from standard party buffs like Haste, Protect, or Bard songs. This limits how much your support Trusts can enhance her.
  • Plant Abilities — She uses unique plant-based abilities that other Trusts do not have, giving her a distinctive combat style.

When to Use Rosulatia

Rosulatia is a niche pick, primarily for players who enjoy the novelty of having a plant creature casting magic in their party. Her earth-element focus makes her useful against lightning-weak enemies, but a standard Black Mage Trust can cast earth spells and much more. Her creature-type limitations on receiving buffs hold her back compared to humanoid mage Trusts. She is best as a collection piece or a fun alternative when you want something different.
