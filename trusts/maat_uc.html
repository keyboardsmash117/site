Maat (UC)

Melee Damage Dealer — Monk

Who is Maat (UC)?

Maat is the legendary old man of Jeuno, famous as the gatekeeper who tests adventurers before they can break the level 70 cap. Despite his age, he is one of the most powerful fighters in Vana'diel. His Unity Concord version is an enhanced melee combatant with stronger stats and improved AI.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobMonk (MNK)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionGeneral
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Hundred Fists — rapid flurry of punches for burst damage
  • Asuran Fists — powerful multi-hit weapon skill
  • Chakra — self-healing to sustain in combat
  • Counter — retaliates against melee attacks automatically
  • Enhanced Stats — the UC version hits significantly harder than base Maat

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Chakra, Counterstance, Impetus

When to Use Maat (UC)

Maat (UC) is a solid melee damage dealer with the Monk's natural toughness. He is a fan-favorite trust thanks to his iconic status in FFXI lore — the old man who humbled every adventurer at level 70. His UC version makes him a viable damage dealer for general content. A fun and effective pick for your party.
