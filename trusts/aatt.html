Ark Angel TT

Ranged Damage Dealer — Ranger / Ninja

Who is Ark Angel TT?

Ark Angel TT is the Tarutaru Ark Angel from the Rise of the Zilart expansion. Despite the tiny stature typical of the Tarutaru race, this Ark Angel is a deadly Ranger who attacks from a distance. The Ark Angels are five celestial warriors who guard the Gates of Paradise — iconic bosses from the Zilart mission storyline that players can later summon as powerful trusts.

What They Do

RoleRanged Damage Dealer
Main JobRanger (RNG)
Sub JobNinja (NIN)
ExpansionRise of the Zilart
How to ObtainSpecial unlock through Zilart storyline content

Key Abilities

  • Ranged attacks — consistent damage from a safe distance
  • Barrage — fires multiple shots in rapid succession for burst damage
  • Sharpshot — increases ranged accuracy
  • Shadowbind — roots the enemy in place
  • Utsusemi — from Ninja sub, creates shadow images that absorb hits for survivability

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Last Resort
  • Spells: Stun, Aspir, None family
  • Passive: +30 Magic Attack, +30 Magic Accuracy

When to Use Ark Angel TT

AATT is a ranged damage dealer who fights from the backline, keeping safe from most melee-range attacks. His Ninja sub gives him Utsusemi shadows for extra survivability. He is a good choice when you want damage that does not require being in melee range, which can be helpful against enemies with dangerous close-range abilities. His Barrage provides strong burst damage on a cooldown.
