Ygnas

Tank — Paladin

Who is Ygnas?

Ygnas is the King of the Sacred City of Adoulin, featured in the Seekers of Adoulin storyline. A noble ruler who leads his people through perilous times, he takes up sword and shield to fight alongside adventurers. His paladin training makes him a stalwart defender on the front lines.

What They Do

RoleTank

How to ObtainComplete SOA missions

Key Abilities

  • Shield Bash — Stuns enemies with a shield strike
  • Flash — Generates enmity and blinds enemies
  • Cure Magic — Self-heals with white magic to stay alive
  • Sentinel/Rampart — Defensive cooldowns that reduce incoming damage

Ability Summary

  • Spells: Haste, Poisona, Paralyna, Blindna, Silena, Stona, Viruna, Cursna, Erase, Cure family, Protectra family, Shellra family

When to Use Ygnas

Ygnas is a dedicated Paladin tank who holds enemy attention with Flash and enmity generation while using defensive cooldowns and self-healing to survive. He is a strong choice when you need a reliable tank trust, especially for content where the tank needs to absorb heavy hits. Pair him with a healer trust for maximum effectiveness.
