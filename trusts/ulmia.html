Ulmia

Support — Bard

Who is Ulmia?

Ulmia is an Elvaan singer from the hidden island of Tavnazia, known for her hauntingly beautiful voice. She is a key companion throughout the Chains of Promathia storyline, using her songs to guide and support the player's journey against the forces of the Emptiness. Her music carries the memories and hopes of Tavnazia's lost people.

Key Abilities

  • Ballad — restores MP to mage party members over time

When to Use Ulmia

Ulmia is a Bard support trust who excels at buffing the party with songs. She is particularly valuable in parties with mage trusts, as her Ballad songs restore MP over time, keeping your healers and nukers fueled. She is an excellent alternative to Joachim, especially when your party composition leans toward magic users.
