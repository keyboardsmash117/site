Zeid II

Melee DD — Dark Knight

Who is Zeid II?

Zeid II is the upgraded version of Zeid — the brooding Galka Dark Knight from the Bastok and Zilart storylines. Despite the "II" label, he isn't strictly better than base Zeid; he's a different build. Zeid II drops most of the dark-magic toolkit in favour of aggressive pure-melee: always-on Souleater and Last Resort stacking, Stun on reactive windows, and Ground Strike as his signature weapon skill. Base Zeid keeps Nether Void, Drain, Absorbs, and Aspir for sustain — Zeid II trades those for raw damage uptime.

Key Abilities

  • Souleater (always on) — converts HP into extra melee damage. Zeid II keeps this up continuously rather than timing it tactically.
  • Last Resort (always on) — always-on attack boost for defense trade. Stacks with Souleater for massive burst uptime.
  • Stun — reactive on enemy weapon skills, TP moves, job abilities, and spell casts. (Base Zeid also has this — it is not a Zeid II exclusive.)
  • Ground Strike — his signature great-sword weapon skill. Triggers a teamwork dialogue when used alongside Lion II.

Notably not on Zeid II's gambit list: Nether Void, Drain, Absorb ACC, Absorb TP, Aspir. Base Zeid has all of those — they are the sustain/utility toolkit he trades away for the always-on Souleater + Last Resort stacking.

When to Use Zeid II

Zeid II is the pure-melee Dark Knight pick — you get maximum aggression with both signature JAs running at all times, plus reactive Stun on the dangerous enemy moves. He trades away base Zeid's self-sustain (Drain / Absorbs / Aspir) and Nether Void's dark-magic burst.

Pick Zeid II when the fight rewards sustained melee damage and your healer can keep him alive through the defense drop. Pick base Zeid when you want the reactive Stun plus full dark-magic sustain. Both are Set 1 Login Points trusts — having both on hand lets you pick the right one per fight.
