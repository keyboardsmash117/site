Zeid II

Melee DD — Dark Knight

Who is Zeid II?

Zeid II is the upgraded version of Zeid, the brooding Galka Dark Knight from the Bastok and Zilart storylines. This version represents Zeid at his full power — a battle-hardened warrior who has confronted the Shadow Lord and emerged stronger. He retains all of his dark knight abilities but with improved AI and more devastating attacks.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD
Main JobDark Knight (DRK)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionBastok / Rise of the Zilart
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 5)

Key Abilities

  • Last Resort — the same big attack boost at the cost of defense, but Zeid II uses it more intelligently than his base version.
  • Souleater — converts HP into extra melee damage, same as base Zeid but with better timing.
  • Stun — Zeid II prioritizes using Stun to interrupt dangerous enemy abilities. This is his biggest upgrade over base Zeid. When an enemy starts charging a powerful attack, Zeid II will attempt to stop it with Stun, protecting the whole party.
  • Absorb spells — improved dark magic usage for draining enemy stats and HP.

When to Use Zeid II

Zeid II is superior to base Zeid in every way — better damage, smarter AI, and the crucial addition of Stun. If you have both versions unlocked, always pick Zeid II. His Stun ability makes him especially valuable against enemies with dangerous TP moves or spellcasts, since he can interrupt them before they go off. He is one of the better Dark Knight trusts available and a strong melee DD choice for any party.
