Elivira

Ranged DD — Ranger / Warrior Hybrid

Who is Elivira?

Elivira is an Elvaan from the Kingdom of San d'Oria who combines Ranger marksmanship with Warrior toughness. She is a hybrid fighter who can engage both at range and in melee, making her more versatile than pure Rangers. Her Warrior sub job gives her extra offensive options up close.

What They Do

RoleRanged DD (hybrid melee/ranged)
Main JobRanger (RNG)
Sub JobWarrior (WAR)
ExpansionSan d'Oria
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 3)

Key Abilities

  • Ranged Attacks — Elivira uses her bow to attack from a distance, dealing consistent damage from the back line like any Ranger.
  • Melee Combat — Unlike pure Rangers, Elivira can also fight in melee range. Her Warrior sub job gives her access to melee abilities, making her effective at any distance.
  • Berserk — From her WAR sub job, she can boost her attack power. This works for both melee and can indirectly benefit her overall damage output.
  • Weapon Skills — She uses both ranged and melee weapon skills depending on her current combat range, giving her flexibility in how she deals damage.

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Barrage, Double Shot, Berserk, Decoy Shot

When to Use Elivira

Elivira is a good pick when you want a Ranger who can handle herself if an enemy closes the distance. Pure Rangers can struggle when enemies get close, but Elivira's WAR sub job means she can fight effectively in melee too. She is a versatile damage dealer that adapts to the situation. Use her when you want ranged damage with a melee fallback, or when you just need a solid all-around damage Trust.
