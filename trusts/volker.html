Volker

Melee DD — Warrior

Who is Volker?

Volker is a legendary Hume Warrior and the hero of the Republic of Bastok. He is the leader of the Mythril Musketeers, Bastok's elite military unit, and plays a pivotal role in the nation's mission storyline. His strength and leadership have made him one of the most respected figures in the Republic.

Key Abilities

  • Warcry — boosts the attack power of the entire party for increased damage output
  • Berserk — greatly increases his own attack at the cost of defense
  • Great Sword weapon skills — delivers powerful multi-hit weapon skills for burst damage

When to Use Volker

Volker is a strong melee DD trust whose Warcry ability benefits the entire party's physical damage output. He is particularly effective in parties with multiple melee damage dealers, as Warcry stacks with other attack buffs. A solid choice for Bastok players who want reliable damage with party-wide utility.
