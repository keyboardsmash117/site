Shantotto

Nuker — Black Mage

Who is Shantotto?

Professor Shantotto is the most famous Tarutaru in all of Vana'diel and a former minister of the Orastery in Windurst. Despite her diminutive stature, she is widely regarded as one of the most powerful mages alive, with a personality and laugh to match. Her infamous "Ohohoho!" is recognized across every nation.

What They Do

RoleNuker (magic damage dealer)
Main JobBlack Mage (BLM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWindurst (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (Windurst)

Key Abilities

  • Magic Burst priority — prioritises casting on skillchain windows, firing the matching-element burst for 2–3× damage.
  • Hate-aware casting — she stops casting the moment she pulls top enmity, so she doesn't get gibbed. Waits for the tank to re-establish aggro, then resumes.
  • Elemental nuke rotation — cycles highest-tier tier spells when MB isn't available.
  • 10% Magic Haste baseline — faster recast on every spell.
  • No auto-attack, no movement — she plants herself at range and never melees.

Tier Upgrades

Shantotto uses the Scythe-wide "tiered stat multiplier" system. Her base Magic Attack and Magic Accuracy scale per tier — the bigger the tier, the bigger the nuke.

TierMATT / MACC scaling
Base1.0× base (~+10 at level 99)
Tier I1.5× base
Tier II2.0× base
Tier III (MAX)2.5× base — noticeably harder nukes even on enemies without weaknesses.

When to Use Shantotto

Shantotto is the default nuker for early- to mid-game. Her hate-aware AI makes her safer than aggressive casters like D. Shantotto, and her Magic Burst priority makes her the go-to nuker in skillchain parties. Upgraded through tiers she stays competitive well into endgame. The more powerful Shantotto II is available via Login Points Set 5 for further burst, and D. Shantotto trades safety for raw damage — but base Shantotto is a perfectly fine pick with the tier upgrades done.
