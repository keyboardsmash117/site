Gessho

Tank / Melee DD — Ninja

Who is Gessho?

Gessho is a Yagudo ninja who serves as a key figure in the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline. Despite being a beastman, he is an honorable warrior who follows his own code. His mastery of ninjutsu makes him both a capable fighter and a resilient defender.

What They Do

RoleTank / Melee Damage Dealer
Main JobNinja (NIN)

How to ObtainComplete TOAU missions

Key Abilities

  • Utsusemi: Ichi/Ni — Creates shadow images that absorb incoming attacks
  • Dual Wield — Attacks with two weapons for increased damage output
  • Ninjutsu — Casts elemental ninjutsu for magic damage
  • Migawari — Absorbs a killing blow with a shadow substitute

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Provoke, Yonin
  • Spells: Utsusemi family, Kurayami family, Hojo family
  • Passive: +30 Magic Evasion, +15 Enmity
  • Tank enmity siphon (Scythe-custom: drains enmity from party onto this trust every ~3s while engaged)

When to Use Gessho

Gessho works as an evasion-based tank thanks to Utsusemi shadows, which let him avoid damage entirely rather than absorbing it. He is especially useful when you need a tank that can also contribute meaningful melee damage. Pair him with a healer trust for best results.
