Selh'teus

Unique Support — NPC

Who is Selh'teus?

Selh'teus is a mysterious Zilart prince who plays a pivotal role in the Chains of Promathia storyline. An ancient being with ties to the dawn of Vana'diel's history, he possesses unique powers that transcend conventional job classifications. His enigmatic nature makes him one of the most lore-significant trusts available.

Key Abilities

  • Unique Magic — Uses special abilities not tied to any standard job
  • Party Support — Provides healing and buffing to party members
  • Damage Dealing — Capable of dealing magical damage to enemies
  • Resilience — Possesses above-average survivability for a support trust

When to Use Selh'teus

Selh'teus is a unique trust who does not conform to standard job roles. He provides a mix of support and damage that can fill gaps in your party composition. His unconventional abilities make him interesting to experiment with, though dedicated healers or damage dealers may outperform him in their specific roles.
