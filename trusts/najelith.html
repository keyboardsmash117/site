Najelith

Ranged DD — Ranger

Who is Najelith?

Najelith is a dedicated archer from the Treasures of Aht Urhgan expansion. She is a pure Ranger who stays at maximum distance from enemies, raining arrows down without ever engaging in close combat. Her discipline and focus make her a reliable ranged damage source.

What They Do

RoleRanged DD (damage dealer)
Main JobRanger (RNG)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 1)

Key Abilities

  • Pure Ranged Combat — Najelith stays at range at all times. She never runs into melee, making her one of the safest damage Trusts against enemies with dangerous close-range attacks.
  • Ranged Weapon Skills — She uses bow-based weapon skills for heavy burst damage from a distance. These fire when her TP gauge fills up from regular ranged attacks.
  • Barrage — A Ranger ability that fires multiple arrows in rapid succession. When Barrage activates, she unloads several shots at once for a huge damage spike.
  • Steady Damage — Rangers provide consistent, reliable damage throughout a fight. They do not have the peaks and valleys of melee fighters who might get hit and stunned.

When to Use Najelith

Najelith is a straightforward ranged damage dealer available in Set 1, making her one of the first Trusts you can acquire. She is a solid pick for any party that needs safe, consistent damage from the back line. Her pure ranged approach means she avoids almost all melee-range danger. Pair her with a tank and healer, and she will contribute reliable damage without ever being in harm's way.
