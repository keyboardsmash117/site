Sylvie (UC)

Support — Geomancer

Who is Sylvie (UC)?

Sylvie is a Hume Geomancer from the Seekers of Adoulin expansion. Her Unity Concord version is widely regarded as one of the best support trusts in the game thanks to her powerful Indi and Geo auras that passively buff the party and debuff enemies. Unity Concord trusts are upgraded variants with superior AI obtained through the Unity system.

What They Do

RoleSupport / Aura Buffer
Main JobGeomancer (GEO)
Sub JobWhite Mage (WHM)
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Indi-spells — auras centered on herself that buff nearby party members (Haste, Refresh, Accuracy, etc.)
  • Geo-spells — places a Luopan (bubble) on the battlefield that debuffs enemies or buffs allies in range
  • Cure spells — backup healing from White Mage sub job
  • Bolster — dramatically increases the potency of her geomancy auras
  • Enhanced Aura AI — intelligently picks the best Indi/Geo combination for the situation

Ability Summary

  • Spells: Cure, Erase, Haste, Cure family, None family, Indi Buff family
  • Passive: +180 Indi Duration, +50 Regain, +3 Geomancy Bonus

When to Use Sylvie (UC)

Sylvie (UC) is an exceptional support trust for any content. Her geomancy auras provide passive buffs and debuffs that stack with other sources, making the entire party stronger just by having her present. She is especially powerful in difficult fights where the accuracy, attack, or defense auras make a noticeable difference. One of the most recommended trusts for endgame content.
