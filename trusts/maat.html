Maat

Melee DD — Monk

Who is Maat?

Maat is the legendary old man who has tested countless adventurers at level 75 with his infamous Limit Break challenges. A master of all jobs, he is one of the most iconic NPCs in all of FFXI. As a trust, he mimics your current job's fighting style and brings decades of combat experience to the field.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobMonk (MNK)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionGeneral
How to ObtainRecords of Eminence. Also has a Unity Concord (UC) version.

Key Abilities

  • Hundred Fists — Dramatically increases attack speed for a short duration
  • Combo/Asuran Fists — Powerful hand-to-hand weapon skills
  • Chakra — Self-heal that restores HP based on VIT
  • Boost — Builds up attack power for the next strike

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Mantra, Formless Strikes, Perfect Counter

When to Use Maat

Maat is one of the easier trusts to obtain through Records of Eminence, making him a solid early-game melee option. His Hundred Fists provides excellent burst damage windows. He is a reliable damage dealer for general content, though specialized trusts may outperform him in harder fights.
