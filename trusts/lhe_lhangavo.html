Lhe Lhangavo

Melee DD — Monk

Who is Lhe Lhangavo?

Lhe Lhangavo is a skilled Mithra martial artist from the Seekers of Adoulin storyline. She fights with her bare fists, embodying the Monk job's philosophy of turning the body itself into a weapon. Her speed and discipline make her a formidable close-range fighter.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD (damage dealer)
Main JobMonk (MNK)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 2)

Key Abilities

  • Chakra — A self-heal ability unique to Monks. Lhe Lhangavo restores her own HP based on her VIT stat, letting her patch herself up without relying on a healer for minor damage.
  • Dodge — Temporarily increases her evasion, making enemies miss their attacks more often. Monks are naturally agile, and Dodge pushes that even further.
  • Impetus — A powerful buff that increases her critical hit rate with each consecutive hit. Since Monks attack very fast with their fists, this stacks up quickly and leads to huge damage spikes.
  • Fast Attacks — Monks fight with hand-to-hand weapons (or bare fists), which attack much faster than swords or axes. This means more hits per minute and faster TP gain for weapon skills.

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Dodge, Chakra, Impetus, Focus, Provoke

When to Use Lhe Lhangavo

Lhe Lhangavo is a solid pick when you want fast, consistent melee damage. Monks excel at sustained damage thanks to their rapid attack speed, and Impetus makes her critical hits pile up fast. Her Chakra self-heal means she needs less attention from your healer, which is a nice bonus. She works well in any standard party alongside a tank and healer.
