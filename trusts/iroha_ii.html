Iroha II

Melee DD — Samurai

Who is Iroha II?

Iroha II is the upgraded version of Iroha from Rhapsodies of Vana'diel. Representing Iroha at her full potential, this version is significantly stronger with enhanced abilities and unique techniques. She is widely considered one of the best damage-dealing trusts in the game.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobSamurai (SAM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionRhapsodies of Vana'diel
How to ObtainUpgrade from base Iroha via ROV missions

Key Abilities

  • Meikyo Shisui — Instant TP fill for back-to-back weapon skills
  • Enhanced Meditate — Even faster TP generation than base Iroha
  • Unique Weapon Skills — Access to special techniques not available to the base version
  • Third Eye — Anticipates and avoids incoming attacks for survivability

When to Use Iroha II

Iroha II is one of the strongest DD trusts available and should always be used over base Iroha if you have access. Her enhanced TP generation and unique weapon skills give her exceptional sustained and burst damage. She is a top-tier pick for virtually any content where you need melee damage.
