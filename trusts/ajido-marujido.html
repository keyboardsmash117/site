Ajido-Marujido

Nuker — Black Mage

Who is Ajido-Marujido?

Ajido-Marujido is the Minister of the Orastery in Windurst, the magical research institution of the Federation. This Tarutaru prodigy is brilliant but reckless, often rushing headlong into danger in pursuit of knowledge. He is a central figure in the Windurst mission storyline and one of the most powerful mages in the Federation.

What They Do

RoleNuker (magic damage dealer)
Main JobBlack Mage (BLM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWindurst (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (Windurst)

Key Abilities

  • Elemental Magic — casts a wide range of elemental nukes targeting enemy weaknesses
  • Ancient Magic — unleashes high-tier spells for devastating burst damage
  • Manafont — eliminates MP cost for a period, enabling a rapid-fire nuke barrage

Ability Summary

  • Spells: Cure family, None family, Slow family
  • Tier-scaling stat: Magic Attack, Magic Accuracy (1.0× → 2.5×)

When to Use Ajido-Marujido

Ajido-Marujido is another strong Black Mage trust from Windurst, serving as an alternative or complement to Shantotto. He performs well against enemies weak to magic and is a good choice when you need ranged magical damage to avoid melee-range dangers. Pair him with a tank and healer for safe, consistent magic damage output.
