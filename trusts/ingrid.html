Ingrid

Healer — White Mage

Who is Ingrid?

Ingrid is a healer from the Crystal War era encountered during the Wings of the Goddess storyline. She plays a significant role in the wartime narrative, tending to the wounded on the battlefield. Her dedication to healing makes her a reliable support trust.

What They Do

RoleHealer
Main JobWhite Mage (WHM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWings of the Goddess
How to ObtainComplete WOTG missions. Upgrade to Ingrid II available via login points (becomes DD).

Key Abilities

  • Cure IV/V — Strong single-target healing spells
  • Curaga — Area-of-effect healing for the party
  • Status Removal — Casts Erase, Paralyna, Silena, and other status cures
  • Protect/Shell — Buffs party with defensive enhancements

Ability Summary

  • Spells: Haste, Cursna, Cure family, Banish family
  • Passive: +3 Refresh

When to Use Ingrid

Ingrid is a straightforward healer trust who keeps the party alive with consistent cures and status removal. She is a good choice when you need a dedicated White Mage and already have damage covered. Her upgraded version, Ingrid II, shifts to a damage dealer role if you prefer offense.
