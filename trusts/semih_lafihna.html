Semih Lafihna

Ranged DD — Ranger

Who is Semih Lafihna?

Semih Lafihna is a Mithra ranger from Windurst who serves as one of the Sibyl Guards, elite protectors of the Star Sibyl. She is a skilled archer who attacks exclusively from range, staying safely behind the front line while peppering enemies with arrows.

What They Do

RoleRanged DD (damage dealer)
Main JobRanger (RNG)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWindurst
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 4)

Key Abilities

  • Ranged Attacks — Rangers attack from a distance using bows, crossbows, or guns. This means they stay out of melee range, avoiding most physical attacks from enemies. They are safer than melee fighters but need to maintain proper distance.
  • High TP Gain — Semih Lafihna generates TP quickly through her ranged attacks, letting her use weapon skills frequently. More weapon skills means more burst damage throughout a fight.
  • Ranged Weapon Skills — She uses powerful ranged weapon skills like Sidewinder and other bow-based attacks that deal heavy damage from a safe distance.
  • Sharpshot — A Ranger ability that increases ranged accuracy, ensuring her arrows hit their mark even against evasive enemies.

When to Use Semih Lafihna

Semih Lafihna is a pure ranged damage dealer. She never engages in melee, making her very safe against enemies that have dangerous close-range attacks or area effects centered on themselves. Her high TP gain means she uses weapon skills often, contributing consistent burst damage. She is a good introductory Ranger Trust that shows new players how ranged combat works in FFXI: stay back, shoot, and let the tank handle the front line.
