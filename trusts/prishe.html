Prishe

Melee DD — Monk

Who is Prishe?

Prishe is an Elvaan girl from the hidden island of Tavnazia who was cursed with immortality by the power of the Mothercrystal. Despite her youthful appearance, she has lived for decades and carries a fierce, headstrong personality. She is one of the central characters of the Chains of Promathia storyline and fights with her fists as a Monk.

What They Do

RoleMelee damage dealer
Main JobMonk (MNK)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionChains of Promathia
How to ObtainStory mission (Chains of Promathia)

Key Abilities

  • Combo weapon skills — delivers rapid multi-hit hand-to-hand weapon skills
  • Hundred Fists — dramatically increases attack speed for a devastating burst of damage
  • Chakra — self-heals to stay in the fight longer without relying on the healer

When to Use Prishe

Prishe is a strong melee DD trust with high survivability thanks to Chakra self-healing. Her fast attack speed means she builds TP quickly and uses weapon skills often. She is a great choice for Chains of Promathia content and beyond. An upgraded version, Prishe II, is available from Login Points and is one of the strongest DD trusts in the game.
