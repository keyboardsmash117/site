Mildaurion

Melee DD — Samurai

Who is Mildaurion?

Mildaurion is a character from the Seekers of Adoulin expansion. A skilled samurai warrior, she is known for her role in the colonization efforts of Ulbuka. What makes her special as a trust is not just her damage, but her ability to open skillchains — a critical combat mechanic that can dramatically increase your party's overall damage output.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD (Skillchain Opener)
Main JobSamurai (SAM)

Key Abilities

  • Skillchain Opener — Mildaurion is specifically programmed to use her weapon skill first in a sequence, setting up the "opening" half of a skillchain. She times her weapon skills to create windows for a partner trust to follow up.
  • Samurai abilities — Hasso (attack speed boost) and Meditate (instant TP) help her build TP quickly so she can open skillchains frequently.

Understanding Skillchains

Skillchains are one of the most powerful mechanics in FFXI. Here is how they work: when one character uses a weapon skill, there is a brief window (about 3-6 seconds) where a second character can use a compatible weapon skill to create a "skillchain." The skillchain produces a burst of bonus elemental damage on top of both weapon skills' damage. Think of it like a combo system — the first weapon skill "opens" the chain, and the second "closes" it. Not all weapon skills are compatible; they need to share specific elemental properties. Mildaurion is designed to be the opener in these combos.

Ability Summary

  • Passive: +100% max MP
  • Tier-scaling stat: Double Attack (1.0× → 2.5×)

When to Use Mildaurion

Use Mildaurion when you want to set up skillchains. She pairs perfectly with Morimar, who is designed to close the skillchains that Mildaurion opens. Together, they create a reliable opener-closer combo that produces consistent skillchain damage. Even without a closer partner, Mildaurion is a solid Samurai DD on her own. But her real value is in that skillchain synergy.
