Arciela II

Magic Hybrid — Red Mage / Black Mage

Who is Arciela II?

Arciela II is the upgraded version of Princess Arciela from the Seekers of Adoulin storyline. This more powerful form represents Arciela at the height of her magical abilities, wielding stronger spells and more potent enfeebling magic than her base version.

What They Do

RoleMagic Hybrid
Main JobRed Mage (RDM) / Black Mage (BLM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
How to ObtainUpgrade from base Arciela via SOA missions

Key Abilities

  • Enhanced Enfeebling — Stronger debuffs with higher landing rates
  • Higher-Tier Nukes — Access to more powerful offensive magic
  • Cure IV/V — Improved healing compared to base Arciela
  • Refresh II/Haste — Enhanced party buffs for MP and speed

Ability Summary

  • Spells: Haste family, Refresh family, Protect family, Shell family, Slow family, Paralyze family, None family
  • Passive: +30 Fast Cast

When to Use Arciela II

Arciela II is strictly better than base Arciela in every way. If you have access to both, always use the upgraded version. She remains a versatile magic hybrid but with notably stronger spells and more reliable enfeebles. She excels in content where debuffing enemies is critical.
