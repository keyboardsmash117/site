King of Hearts

Support — Red Mage / White Mage

Who is King of Hearts?

King of Hearts is a Cardian, one of the magical card-soldier automatons created by the Federation of Windurst. Cardians are animated playing cards brought to life through Windurstian magic, and they serve as soldiers and servants throughout the nation. King of Hearts appears during the Chains of Promathia storyline and is notable for being more self-aware than most Cardians.

What They Do

RoleSupport / Buffer

Key Abilities

  • +20 Fast Cast baseline — buffs roll out quickly.
  • Erase + full -na suite (Poisona, Paralyna, Blindna, Silena, Cursna) — top-priority cleanse.
  • Cure at < 50% HP — backup healing for the party.
  • Haste, Refresh, Phalanx, Protect, Shell — master (you) + party coverage.
  • Dia on target.
  • Magic Burst on skillchain windows — opportunistic nuking when burst is available.

Tier Upgrades

King of Hearts is a tier-upgradeable trust. Upgrading turns him from a solid support into a top-tier Red Mage buffer with Refresh III and Temper.

TierUnlocks
BaseFull RDM/WHM toolkit above, +20 Fast Cast.
Tier IRefresh III spell added, +30 Fast Cast total.
Tier II+40 Fast Cast total, +20 MND.
Tier III (MAX)Temper spell added (party-wide Multi-Strikes buff), +50 Fast Cast total.

When to Use King of Hearts

King of Hearts and Koru-Moru fill similar RDM-buffer roles with different specialisations — Koru-Moru brings Dispel/Slow/Distract, King of Hearts brings more cleanse (Erase + -na suite), native Phalanx, and at Tier III the rare party-wide Temper buff. Use either as your main support; both at once is overkill unless you're stripping multiple enemies.
