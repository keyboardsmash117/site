Ark Angel MR

Magic Damage Dealer — Black Mage / Red Mage

Who is Ark Angel MR?

Ark Angel MR is the Mithra Ark Angel from the Rise of the Zilart expansion. The Ark Angels are five celestial warriors created to guard the Gates of Paradise, serving as fearsome bosses in the Zilart storyline. As a trust, AAMR brings devastating Black Mage nukes supplemented by Red Mage utility, making her a potent magic damage dealer.

What They Do

RoleMagic Damage Dealer
Main JobBlack Mage (BLM)
Sub JobRed Mage (RDM)
ExpansionRise of the Zilart
How to ObtainSpecial unlock through Zilart storyline content

Key Abilities

  • Elemental nukes — powerful tier III and IV black magic spells
  • Ancient Magic — high-damage spells for burst damage
  • Enfeebling Magic — debuffs from Red Mage sub to weaken enemies
  • Elemental Seal — ensures the next spell lands without resist
  • Manafont — casts spells without MP cost for a burst window

When to Use Ark Angel MR

AAMR is your go-to Ark Angel for magic damage. She excels at nuking enemies from range while her Red Mage sub adds some debuffing utility. She works best in parties where a tank holds the enemy's attention, letting her cast freely. Pair her with a healer and tank for a well-rounded setup. She is especially effective against enemies weak to magic or resistant to physical damage.
