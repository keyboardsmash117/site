Iroha

Melee DD — Samurai

Who is Iroha?

Iroha is the heroine of Rhapsodies of Vana'diel, the grand finale storyline of FFXI. A samurai from a dark future, she travels back in time to find her master and prevent the destruction of Vana'diel. Her unwavering determination and skill with the katana make her one of the most beloved characters in FFXI history.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobSamurai (SAM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionRhapsodies of Vana'diel
How to ObtainComplete ROV missions. Has upgrade Iroha II.

Key Abilities

  • Meikyo Shisui — Fills TP instantly for immediate weapon skill use
  • Meditate — Rapidly generates TP for frequent weapon skills
  • Third Eye — Anticipates and evades incoming attacks
  • Weapon Skills — Uses powerful katana weapon skills including multi-hit techniques

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Hasso, Third Eye, Hagakure, Meditate
  • Spells: Protectra family, Shellra family

When to Use Iroha

Iroha is an excellent melee damage dealer who generates TP quickly and uses it for devastating weapon skills. Her Meditate ability means she fires off weapon skills more frequently than most trusts. She is one of the strongest DD trusts available and works well in virtually any party composition. Her upgraded version, Iroha II, is even more powerful.
