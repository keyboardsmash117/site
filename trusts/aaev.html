Ark Angel EV

Healer — White Mage

Who is Ark Angel EV?

Ark Angel EV is the Elvaan Ark Angel from the Rise of the Zilart expansion. The Ark Angels are five celestial warriors who guard the Gates of Paradise — powerful bosses in the Zilart mission storyline. As a trust, AAEV serves as a White Mage healer, channeling divine magic to keep your party alive. She is one of the more unique healer trusts thanks to her Ark Angel origin.

What They Do

RoleHealer
Main JobWhite Mage (WHM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionRise of the Zilart
How to ObtainSpecial unlock through Zilart storyline content

Key Abilities

  • Cure IV / Cure V — strong single-target healing
  • Curaga — area healing for multiple party members
  • Protect / Shell — defensive buffs for the party
  • Raise — revives fallen party members
  • Holy — light-element damage spell for contributing offense between heals

When to Use Ark Angel EV

AAEV is a solid healer trust with the prestige of being an Ark Angel. She handles standard healing duties well and can keep a party going through most content. While dedicated healer trusts like Apururu (UC) may edge her out for harder content, AAEV is a reliable choice and a cool addition to any party. If you enjoy the Zilart storyline, having an Ark Angel heal your party is a fitting reward.
