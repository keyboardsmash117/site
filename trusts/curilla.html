Curilla

Tank — Paladin

Who is Curilla?

Curilla V Mecru is the captain of the Temple Knights of San d'Oria, one of the most prestigious military orders in the kingdom. A Hume woman of remarkable skill, she is fiercely dedicated to protecting San d'Oria and plays a central role throughout the nation's mission storyline.

What They Do

RoleTank with healing support
Main JobPaladin (PLD)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSan d'Oria (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (San d'Oria)

Key Abilities

  • Provoke — reactive whenever she doesn't have top enmity.
  • Flash — enmity spell + blind on the target.
  • Sentinel — fires at < 60% HP for emergency mitigation.
  • Cure — heals party below 75% HP, backup for your healer.
  • Baseline mods: 5% DT reduction, +15 Enmity, +30% MP, +25 Cure Potency.
  • Tank Enmity Siphon — passive Scythe-custom mechanic that drains enmity from party members onto her every few seconds while engaged.

Tier Upgrades

Curilla scales dramatically with tier upgrades — from a starter tank into a high-tier PLD with full defensive JA rotation and doubled cure potency.

TierUnlocks
BaseProvoke, Flash, Sentinel, Cure, 5% DT, +15 Enmity, +25 Cure Potency.
Tier I+30 Defense, Palisade JA added (fires at < 70% HP for magic mitigation).
Tier II+60 Defense total, Rampart JA added (party-wide magic shield at < 60% HP).
Tier III (MAX)+100 Defense total, Shield Bash always active (enemy-stun JA), +50 Cure Potency total.

When to Use Curilla

Curilla is your go-to early-game tank, and with upgrades she scales up nicely into mid-game content. Her self-cure and MP pool let her survive longer than pure tanks when healer support is thin. Tier III gives her the full defensive JA rotation (Sentinel + Palisade + Rampart + Shield Bash) that makes her one of the stronger non-August tank options.

If you're willing to grind out Login Points, August outclasses her for pure damage soaking — but August costs 100 pts in Set 5, while Curilla is accessible much earlier via nation quests.
