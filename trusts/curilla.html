Curilla

Tank — Paladin

Who is Curilla?

Curilla V Mecru is the captain of the Temple Knights of San d'Oria, one of the most prestigious military orders in the kingdom. A Hume woman of remarkable skill, she is fiercely dedicated to protecting San d'Oria and plays a central role throughout the nation's mission storyline.

What They Do

RoleTank with healing support
Main JobPaladin (PLD)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSan d'Oria (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (San d'Oria)

Key Abilities

  • Flash — blinds enemies and generates enmity to hold their attention
  • Cure — heals herself and party members, reducing pressure on your main healer
  • Sentinel — greatly reduces incoming damage for a short time during dangerous moments

When to Use Curilla

Curilla is one of the best early-game tank trusts thanks to her combination of solid enmity generation and self-healing. She can keep herself alive longer than pure tanks, which is especially useful when your healer trust is struggling with MP. She pairs well with any healer and DD combination for general adventuring.
