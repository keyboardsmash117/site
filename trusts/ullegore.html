Ullegore

Magic DD — Black Mage

Who is Ullegore?

Ullegore is a dark and enigmatic mage from the Seekers of Adoulin expansion. He is a dedicated Black Mage who focuses entirely on offensive magic, never engaging in melee combat. His single-minded devotion to destruction makes him a reliable source of magical damage.

What They Do

RoleMagic DD (nuker)
Main JobBlack Mage (BLM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 2)

Key Abilities

  • Elemental Nukes — Ullegore casts high-tier elemental spells (Fire, Blizzard, Thunder, etc.) to deal heavy magic damage. He stays at range and never runs into melee.
  • No Melee — Unlike some mage Trusts that occasionally swing a staff, Ullegore is a pure caster. He stands back and channels spells continuously.
  • Magic Burst — He can capitalize on skillchains by casting matching elemental spells for bonus damage. Magic bursts are where Black Mages really shine.
  • Mana Management — Like all Black Mages, Ullegore uses MP (mana points) to cast spells. He paces his casting to avoid running out of MP mid-fight.

When to Use Ullegore

Ullegore is a solid Black Mage Trust for when you want consistent magical damage. He does not have the star power of Shantotto II, but he is a reliable nuker who does his job without fuss. Use him when you want magic damage and already have Shantotto II in your party, or when you prefer a different Set for your Login Points purchases. He needs a tank in front of him since mages are fragile.
