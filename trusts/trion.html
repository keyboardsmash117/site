Trion

Tank — Paladin

Who is Trion?

Prince Trion is the Crown Prince of the Kingdom of San d'Oria and elder brother to Prince Pieuje. A proud and noble Elvaan, he leads from the front lines and is deeply involved in the defense of his kingdom. He is a central figure throughout the San d'Oria mission storyline.

What They Do

RoleTank
Main JobPaladin (PLD)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSan d'Oria (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (San d'Oria)

Key Abilities

  • Flash — blinds enemies and generates strong enmity to hold their attention
  • Invincible — becomes immune to physical damage briefly, useful in emergencies
  • Cure — heals himself and allies, providing backup healing support

When to Use Trion

Trion is a reliable Paladin tank trust from San d'Oria who excels at holding enemy attention and staying alive. His Invincible ability can save the party in dangerous situations. He is a strong choice for any content where you need a dedicated tank, and pairs well with healer trusts like Kupipi or Cherukiki.
