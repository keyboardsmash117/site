Naji

Melee DD — Warrior

Who is Naji?

Naji is a Hume member of the Bastok Musketeer unit, one of the Republic's elite military forces. He is a loyal soldier who plays a key role in the Bastok mission storyline, often serving as the player's point of contact for important assignments from the President.

What They Do

RoleMelee damage dealer
Main JobWarrior (WAR)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionBastok (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (Bastok)

Key Abilities

  • Weapon Skills — uses sword-based weapon skills for consistent melee damage
  • Provoke — can draw enemy attention temporarily, providing some off-tank utility
  • Berserk — boosts attack power at the cost of defense for increased damage output

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Provoke, Berserk, Warcry

When to Use Naji

Naji is a straightforward melee damage dealer available early in the game for Bastok-aligned players. He fills a basic DD role and works fine for leveling and early content. As you progress, you will likely replace him with stronger melee trusts, but he remains a reliable option when you need an extra sword in the party.
