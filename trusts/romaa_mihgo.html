Romaa Mihgo

Melee DD — Thief

Who is Romaa Mihgo?

Romaa Mihgo is the ancestor of the notorious Nanaa Mihgo, encountered during the Wings of the Goddess storyline set in the Crystal War era. Like her descendant, she is a skilled Mithra thief with quick reflexes and a talent for finding treasure on the battlefield.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobThief (THF)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWings of the Goddess
How to ObtainComplete WOTG missions

Key Abilities

  • Sneak Attack — Next attack from behind deals massive damage
  • Trick Attack — Transfers enmity to the party member behind her
  • Steal — Attempts to steal items from enemies
  • Assassin's Charge — Increases critical hit rate temporarily

When to Use Romaa Mihgo

Romaa Mihgo is a good choice when you want a thief-style damage dealer who can position for Sneak Attack burst damage. Her Trick Attack also helps manage enmity by directing hate to your tank trust. She works best in parties where positioning allows her to take advantage of her backstab abilities.
