Kuyin Hathdenna

Ranged Support — Supportive Ranger

Who is Kuyin Hathdenna?

Kuyin Hathdenna is a Mithra from the Wings of the Goddess expansion with a unique approach to combat. Unlike typical Rangers who focus on dealing damage from range, Kuyin takes a more supportive role. She does not auto-attack, instead using her abilities to assist the party in other ways. She is one of the more unconventional Trusts available.

Key Abilities

  • No Auto-Attack — Kuyin Hathdenna does not automatically attack enemies. This is very unusual for a Trust and means her direct damage contribution is minimal.
  • Supportive Actions — She uses her abilities to support the party rather than deal damage. Her contribution is more about utility than raw numbers.
  • Ranged Positioning — She stays at range like a Ranger, keeping herself safe from melee attacks even though she is not shooting.
  • Niche Utility — Her specific support actions are situational. She is not a replacement for a healer, buffer, or damage dealer.

When to Use Kuyin Hathdenna

Kuyin Hathdenna is a very niche Trust. Her lack of auto-attacking means she does not contribute damage in the way most Trusts do. She is primarily a collection pick for players who want every Trust, or for specific situations where her support abilities are useful. For general content, you will get much more value from a standard damage, healing, or tanking Trust. Only summon her if you have a specific reason or just want to experiment.
