Lilisette

DD / Support — Dancer

Who is Lilisette?

Lilisette is the time-traveling dancer and central heroine of the Wings of the Goddess storyline. A spirited young woman who leaps between eras to change the fate of the Crystal War, she combines graceful dance combat with supportive abilities. Her cheerful personality belies her determination to save those she loves.

How to ObtainComplete WOTG missions. Upgrade to Lilisette II available via login points.

Key Abilities

  • Curing Waltz — Heals party members using TP instead of MP
  • Haste Samba — Grants haste to melee attackers through a dance aura
  • Drain Samba — Melee attacks drain HP from enemies
  • Reverse Flourish — Converts finishing moves into TP for weapon skills

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Quickstep, Violent Flourish

When to Use Lilisette

Lilisette is a versatile trust who deals melee damage while providing party support through sambas and waltzes. Her Haste Samba benefits the entire melee lineup, and her Curing Waltz provides emergency healing without needing MP. She is excellent in melee-heavy parties where the extra haste and sustain make a real difference.
