Brygid

NPC — Minimal Combat

Who is Brygid?

Brygid is a scholar and historian from the Seekers of Adoulin expansion. She is more of a lore character than a fighter, serving as a keeper of knowledge about the history of Ulbuka. Her inclusion as a Trust is primarily for collection purposes and fans of the Adoulin storyline.

What They Do

RoleNPC (minimal combat)
Main JobNPC (unique)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 2)

Key Abilities

  • Minimal Combat — Brygid does very little in battle. She is not designed to be a damage dealer, healer, or tank. Her combat contribution is negligible.
  • Lore Character — Her value is in being a recognizable character from the Adoulin storyline rather than a combat-effective Trust.
  • Collection Pick — Some players enjoy collecting every available Trust. Brygid fills a slot in that collection.

When to Use Brygid

Honestly, you should not use Brygid for serious content. She contributes almost nothing in combat. She exists as a collectible Trust for players who want every character available or who are fans of her role in the Adoulin story. If you are building a party to actually fight things, pick any other Trust instead. Brygid is purely for fun and lore appreciation.
