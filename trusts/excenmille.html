Excenmille

Tank — Paladin

Who is Excenmille?

Excenmille is an Elvaan Royal Knight of San d'Oria, a proud member of the kingdom's elite guard. He is a devoted protector of the realm who takes his duties seriously, often appearing throughout the San d'Oria mission storyline as a stalwart ally to the player.

Key Abilities

  • Shield Bash — stuns enemies briefly while maintaining hate
  • Flash — blinds the enemy and generates enmity to keep attention on himself
  • Cure — heals himself to stay alive, reducing strain on your healer

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Provoke, Sentinel
  • Spells: Flash, Cure family
  • Passive: +25 Store TP, 5% DT reduction, +15 Enmity
  • Tank enmity siphon (Scythe-custom: drains enmity from party onto this trust every ~3s while engaged)

When to Use Excenmille

Excenmille is one of the earliest tank trusts available and does a solid job holding enemy attention while your party deals damage. He pairs well with a healer trust like Kupipi or Cherukiki. Note that an upgraded version, Excenmille (S) from the Wings of the Goddess era, is also available and performs better at higher levels.
