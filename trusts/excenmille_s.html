Excenmille (S)

Tank — Paladin

Who is Excenmille (S)?

Excenmille (S) is the Crystal War-era version of Excenmille, the noble Elvaan Paladin from San d'Oria. The "(S)" denotes his appearance during the Wings of the Goddess expansion, which takes players back to the Crystal War period 20 years in the past. He wears period-appropriate gear and fights as a younger, more zealous version of himself.

What They Do

RoleTank

Key Abilities

  • Flash — generates enmity to hold the enemy's attention
  • Shield Bash — stuns enemies briefly
  • Sentinel — reduces incoming damage for a duration
  • Cure spells — self-healing to survive while tanking
  • Provoke — from Warrior sub job, helps maintain hate

When to Use Excenmille (S)

Excenmille (S) is a Paladin tank option for players who have progressed through Wings of the Goddess content. He functions similarly to other PLD tanks but with a Crystal War aesthetic. He is a decent tanking choice for leveling and mid-tier content. If you need a stronger PLD tank, consider Pieuje (UC) or other enhanced tank trusts.
