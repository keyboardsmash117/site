Qultada

Support — Corsair

Who is Qultada?

Qultada is a Corsair captain from the Arrapago pirates, a band of seafaring adventurers operating in the seas around the Empire of Aht Urhgan. He plays a central role in the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline, where he helps you navigate the dangerous political waters of the Near East. He is calm, calculating, and always has a trick up his sleeve.

Key Abilities

Qultada stacks his Phantom Rolls on the party and shoots between them. Rolls last for a limited time but are a unique buff category that stacks with everything else — Haste, Refresh, Protect, you name it.

  • Corsair's Roll (party Exp/Cap bonus) — kept up at all times.
  • Chaos Roll (Melee Attack) — kept up at all times.
  • Hunter's Roll (Party Accuracy) — kept up at all times.
  • Fighter's Roll (Double Attack) — kept up at all times.
  • Triple Shot — self-buff for burst-damage shots.
  • Dark Shot — dispels one buff from the enemy.
  • Ranged Attack — fills TP between rolls and shots.

Tier Upgrades

Qultada's tier upgrades unlock additional Phantom Rolls — each tier adds one more to his rotation.

TierUnlocks
Base4 rolls: Corsair's, Chaos, Hunter's, Fighter's.
Tier IEvoker's Roll added (party Refresh bonus).
Tier IIWizard's Roll added (party Magic Attack bonus).
Tier III (MAX)Warlock's Roll added (party Magic Accuracy bonus). Seven rolls total.

When to Use Qultada

Bring Qultada whenever you want the Corsair buff category layered on top of your existing support. He stacks with every other buff in the game, so the rolls are pure additive power. At Tier III with seven rolls he's the strongest multi-element buffer in the trust pool — strong pick for both melee (Chaos + Fighter's + Hunter's) and magic (Wizard's + Warlock's + Evoker's) parties. Pairs beautifully with Koru-Moru for double support.
