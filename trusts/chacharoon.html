Chacharoon

Melee DD — Thief

Who is Chacharoon?

Chacharoon is a Goblin thief companion from the Seekers of Adoulin storyline. Unlike most Goblins who are hostile, Chacharoon is a friendly rogue who joins your adventures in the wilds of Ulbuka. His keen eye for treasure makes him a unique and useful ally.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobThief (THF)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
How to ObtainComplete SOA missions

Key Abilities

  • Treasure Hunter — Increases drop rates from enemies he attacks
  • Sneak Attack — Deals bonus damage when striking from behind
  • Trick Attack — Transfers enmity to party member behind him
  • Mug — Steals gil from enemies during combat

Ability Summary

  • Passive: +-10% max HP

When to Use Chacharoon

Chacharoon's main draw is Treasure Hunter, which boosts item drop rates from enemies he engages. If you are farming specific drops, he is one of the best trusts to bring along. His melee damage is decent but secondary to his treasure-finding utility.
