Aldo (UC)

Melee Damage Dealer — Thief

Who is Aldo (UC)?

Aldo is the leader of the Tenshodo, the shadowy merchant guild based in Jeuno. As a Thief, he strikes from the shadows with precision. His Unity Concord version enhances his Sneak Attack damage and overall combat effectiveness with improved AI and stats.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobThief (THF)
Sub JobWarrior (WAR)
ExpansionJeuno
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Sneak Attack — deals bonus damage when attacking from behind the target
  • Trick Attack — transfers enmity to the party member behind the Thief
  • Assassin's Charge — enhances critical hit rate
  • Bully — intimidates the target
  • Enhanced Damage — UC version deals noticeably more damage with better Sneak Attack timing

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Bully, Sneak Attack

When to Use Aldo (UC)

Aldo (UC) is a burst damage dealer who excels at Sneak Attack weapon skills from behind the enemy. He works best when a tank is holding the enemy's attention, letting him position for backstabs. The UC upgrade improves his positioning AI and damage output. A good choice when you want a melee DD who can also help with enmity management via Trick Attack.
