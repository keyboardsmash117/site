Sakura

Support — Geomancer

Who is Sakura?

Sakura is an automaton puppet from the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline, connected to Empress Nashmeira's entourage. Like Ovjang and Mnejing, she is a mechanical companion with her own personality. As a trust, she takes on the Geomancer role, providing a passive healing aura to everyone around her.

What They Do

RoleSupport / Buffer (Passive)
Main JobGeomancer (GEO)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 3)

Key Abilities

  • Regen Aura (Constant HP Recovery) — Sakura projects an aura that continuously restores a small amount of HP (Hit Points, your health) to all nearby allies every few seconds. This is like having a passive healing spell running at all times. You do not need to do anything to benefit from it. Just stay near Sakura and your health will slowly tick upward. This does not replace a real healer for big damage, but it smooths out the small hits and keeps everyone topped off between fights.

When to Use Sakura

Sakura is great as a supplement to a real healer trust. Pair her with Apururu or Yoran-Oran (dedicated healers) so the healer handles the big damage while Sakura's passive Regen keeps everyone's HP from dipping between heals. She is especially useful when fighting multiple enemies or when your party takes frequent small hits. For the ultimate passive sustain combo, pair Sakura with Moogle: Sakura provides Regen (HP recovery) and Moogle provides Refresh (MP recovery), giving your party both resources regenerating automatically.
