Mumor II

Magic DD — Dancer Nuker

Who is Mumor II?

Mumor is a famous Tarutaru dancer and pop idol from the Wings of the Goddess expansion, known for her energetic performances and upbeat personality. This upgraded version shifts her from a support-oriented healer to a magical damage dealer. She channels the power of dance into offensive magic, making her one of the more unique Trust configurations.

What They Do

RoleMagic DD (nuker)
Main JobDancer (DNC)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWings of the Goddess
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 5)

Key Abilities

  • Magic Damage — Unlike the standard healer version of Mumor, Mumor II focuses on dealing magical damage. She uses her dance abilities offensively rather than for healing or support.
  • Offensive Dances — Her dances are tuned for damage output. She channels dance steps into damaging attacks rather than healing waltzes.
  • Dancer Utility — She retains some Dancer abilities like Steps that debuff enemies, adding utility alongside her damage.
  • Unique Playstyle — A Dancer that nukes is unusual in FFXI. Mumor II represents one of the more creative Trust designs.

When to Use Mumor II

Mumor II is a niche magic damage Trust. She is fun and unique, but she does not match dedicated Black Mages for raw nuking power. Use her if you already have your core party covered and want something different, or if you are a fan of Mumor from the WOTG storyline. If you need serious magical damage, Shantotto II or another dedicated BLM will serve you better.
