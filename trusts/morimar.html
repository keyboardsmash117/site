Morimar

Melee DD — Samurai

Who is Morimar?

Morimar is a character from the Seekers of Adoulin expansion, a samurai warrior involved in the exploration of Ulbuka. As a trust, Morimar is specifically designed to close skillchains — he watches for other trusts to use weapon skills and then follows up with a compatible weapon skill to complete the combo. He is the perfect partner for Mildaurion.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD (Skillchain Closer)
Main JobSamurai (SAM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 5)

Key Abilities

  • Skillchain Closer — Morimar watches for weapon skills from other party members and follows up with a compatible weapon skill to complete a skillchain. This produces bonus elemental damage on top of both weapon skills.
  • Samurai abilities — Hasso (attack speed boost) and Meditate (instant TP) let him build TP quickly and be ready to close skillchains when the window opens.

The Mildaurion + Morimar Combo

These two trusts are designed to work together. Mildaurion uses a weapon skill to open a skillchain, and Morimar immediately follows up to close it. The result is a reliable stream of skillchain damage on top of their normal weapon skill damage. This is one of the easiest ways to get consistent skillchains in a trust party. If you summon both, they will automatically coordinate their weapon skills without any input from you.

Ability Summary

  • Passive: +hpBoost HP

When to Use Morimar

Morimar shines when paired with Mildaurion or any other trust/player who opens skillchains. Without an opener, he is still a solid Samurai DD, but you are not getting his full value. For maximum damage, always pair him with Mildaurion. This duo is one of the best trust combos for sustained damage output through constant skillchaining.
