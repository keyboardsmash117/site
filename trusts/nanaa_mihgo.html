Nanaa Mihgo

Melee DD — Thief

Who is Nanaa Mihgo?

Nanaa Mihgo is a Mithra and the legendary Cat Burglar of Windurst, known for her cunning and agility. Despite her questionable profession, she plays an important role in the Windurst mission storyline, using her skills to aid the Federation when it matters most.

What They Do

RoleMelee damage dealer
Main JobThief (THF)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionWindurst (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (Windurst)

Key Abilities

  • Sneak Attack — delivers a high-damage strike from behind the target
  • Trick Attack — transfers enmity to the party member behind her, helping your tank hold hate
  • Dagger weapon skills — fast, frequent weapon skills that build damage over time

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Bully, Sneak Attack, Assassins Charge

When to Use Nanaa Mihgo

Nanaa Mihgo provides solid melee damage with the added benefit of Trick Attack, which can help direct enmity to your tank. She is most effective when positioned behind an enemy with a tank in front. A good early-game DD choice for Windurst players who want some utility alongside their damage.
