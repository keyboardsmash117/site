Zazarg

Melee DD — Warrior

Who is Zazarg?

Zazarg is a Galka who serves as one of the five Serpent Generals of the Empire of Aht Urhgan. He is a fierce warrior known for his incredible physical strength and unwavering loyalty to the empire. His raw combat prowess makes him a powerful melee trust.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobWarrior (WAR)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
How to ObtainComplete TOAU missions

Key Abilities

  • Mighty Strikes — All attacks become critical hits for a short duration
  • Warcry — Boosts attack power for nearby party members
  • Berserk — Increases attack at the cost of defense
  • Weapon Skills — Uses powerful great axe weapon skills for burst damage

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Berserk, Warcry, Restraint

When to Use Zazarg

Zazarg is a straightforward melee damage dealer who hits hard and boosts party attack power with Warcry. He is a strong pick when you want raw physical damage output. Just make sure you have a tank trust to keep enemies off your healer, as Zazarg focuses on offense over defense.
