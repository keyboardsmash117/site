Ark Angel GK

Melee Damage Dealer — Samurai / Dragoon

Who is Ark Angel GK?

Ark Angel GK is the Galka Ark Angel from the Rise of the Zilart expansion. The largest and most physically imposing of the five Ark Angels, this celestial warrior wields a great katana with devastating force. The Ark Angels guard the Gates of Paradise and are iconic bosses from the Zilart missions. As a trust, AAGK is a hard-hitting melee damage dealer combining Samurai precision with Dragoon power.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobSamurai (SAM)
Sub JobDragoon (DRG)
ExpansionRise of the Zilart
How to ObtainSpecial unlock through Zilart storyline content

Key Abilities

  • Meditate — rapidly gains TP for frequent weapon skills
  • Hasso — offensive stance that boosts attack speed and strength
  • Sekkanoki — reduces TP cost of the next weapon skill
  • Jump / High Jump — from Dragoon sub, gap-closing attacks that deal extra damage
  • Powerful weapon skills — great katana skills hit hard with Samurai's high TP generation

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Hasso, Meditate, Third Eye, Jump, High Jump

When to Use Ark Angel GK

AAGK is one of the heaviest-hitting melee trusts available. His Samurai main job gives him fast TP and strong weapon skills, while the Dragoon sub adds Jump attacks for extra burst damage. He excels as a primary damage dealer in any party with a tank and healer. Among the Ark Angels, GK is the purest melee powerhouse — use him when you want raw physical damage output.
