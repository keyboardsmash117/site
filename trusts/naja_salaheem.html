Naja Salaheem

Melee DD — Thief

Who is Naja Salaheem?

Naja Salaheem is the president and founder of Salaheem's Sentinels, a mercenary company based in Aht Urhgan. She is your boss throughout the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline — a tough, no-nonsense Mithra businesswoman who is not afraid to get her hands dirty. Despite her demanding personality, she genuinely cares about her mercenaries and will fight alongside them when it matters.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD
Main JobThief (THF)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 1)

Key Abilities

  • Focus — boosts accuracy, helping Naja land her attacks more consistently. Accuracy is important because missed attacks deal zero damage and generate no TP.
  • Dodge — boosts evasion, making enemies miss Naja more often. This is a passive defensive boost that reduces incoming damage over time.
  • Counterstance — greatly increases Naja's chance to counter enemy attacks (hitting back automatically when attacked), but reduces her defense. This turns defense into offense — she takes more damage per hit but deals automatic counter-attacks in return.
  • Peacebreaker — her signature weapon, used for consistent melee damage output.

When to Use Naja Salaheem

Naja is an evasion-focused melee DD. Her combination of Focus, Dodge, and Counterstance makes her surprisingly durable while still dealing solid damage. She works well in parties where you want a DD that does not need constant healing attention. Her counter-attacks also mean she generates TP even while being attacked, leading to more frequent weapon skills.
