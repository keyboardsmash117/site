Valaineral

Tank — Paladin

Who is Valaineral?

Valaineral is an Elvaan Royal Knight of San d'Oria who serves the kingdom with unwavering loyalty. He is a seasoned veteran of the Royal Knights and appears throughout the San d'Oria storyline as a dependable protector. His skill with sword and shield has earned him great respect among the knights.

What They Do

RoleTank
Main JobPaladin (PLD)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSan d'Oria (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (San d'Oria)

Key Abilities

  • Flash — blinds enemies and generates enmity to keep their attention
  • Shield Bash — stuns enemies briefly for damage mitigation
  • Cure — heals himself to reduce pressure on the party's healer

When to Use Valaineral

Valaineral is a solid Paladin tank trust available early for San d'Oria players. He holds enemy attention well and can sustain himself with self-healing. He is a good alternative to Excenmille, Curilla, or Trion if you want variety in your tank selection, and performs reliably through early and mid-game content.
