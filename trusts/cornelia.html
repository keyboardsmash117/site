Cornelia

Healer — White Mage

Who is Cornelia?

Cornelia is a healer NPC available as a special event trust. A dedicated White Mage, she focuses entirely on keeping the party alive with her restorative magic. Her gentle demeanor and steadfast healing make her a welcome addition to any adventuring group.

What They Do

RoleHealer
Main JobWhite Mage (WHM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionGeneral
How to ObtainSpecial event

Key Abilities

  • Cure IV/V — Strong single-target healing to keep party members alive
  • Curaga — Area healing when multiple party members take damage
  • Status Removal — Removes debuffs with Esuna, Paralyna, Silena, and others
  • Protect/Shell — Defensive buffs that reduce physical and magical damage

When to Use Cornelia

Cornelia is a dedicated healer trust who excels at keeping the party healthy. She is a reliable choice whenever you need a White Mage and do not have access to or prefer not to use other healer trusts. Her straightforward healing focus means she will not waste time on offensive actions when the party needs cures.
