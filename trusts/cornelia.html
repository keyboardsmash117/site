Cornelia

Support — Passive Haste Aura

Who is Cornelia?

Cornelia is a special event trust built around passive party support. On Scythe she does not fight or cast like a normal healer; instead, she stays back and provides a Haste aura plus accuracy bonuses while she is active.

What They Do

RoleSupport / Buffer (Passive)
Main JobGeomancer-style aura support
Sub JobNone
ExpansionGeneral
How to ObtainSpecial event

Key Abilities

  • Indi-Haste aura — nearby allies gain a passive Haste effect while Cornelia is active.
  • Accuracy package — the master gains Accuracy, Ranged Accuracy, and Magic Accuracy.
  • No combat actions — Cornelia does not auto-attack or spend time casting; her value is the always-on aura package.

Tier Upgrades

Cornelia is a tier-upgradeable passive support trust. Her Haste aura stays active, and upgrades strengthen the master's accuracy package.

TierUnlocks
BaseIndi-Haste aura, plus +20 Accuracy, +20 Ranged Accuracy, and +20 Magic Accuracy on the master.
Tier I+30 Accuracy, +30 Ranged Accuracy, and +30 Magic Accuracy total.
Tier II+40 Accuracy, +40 Ranged Accuracy, and +40 Magic Accuracy total.
Tier III (MAX)+50 Accuracy, +50 Ranged Accuracy, and +50 Magic Accuracy total.

When to Use Cornelia

Use Cornelia when you want a passive support slot that improves melee, ranged, and magic accuracy without adding another active caster to the party. She is not a healer, so pair her with a real healer trust when incoming damage matters.
