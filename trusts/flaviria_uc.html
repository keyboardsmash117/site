Flaviria (UC)

Support — Red Mage

Who is Flaviria (UC)?

Flaviria is an Elvaan Red Mage from San d'Oria. Her Unity Concord version enhances her already versatile support kit with improved buff timings and stronger magic. Unity Concord trusts are upgraded versions of existing trusts obtained through the Unity system, offering better AI and enhanced abilities.

What They Do

RoleSupport / Buffer
Main JobRed Mage (RDM)
Sub JobWhite Mage (WHM)
ExpansionSan d'Oria
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Haste — speeds up party members' attack and recast timers
  • Refresh — gradually restores MP to party members over time
  • Protect / Shell — defensive buffs for the party
  • Cure spells — backup healing when the main healer is busy
  • Enfeebling Magic — debuffs enemies with Slow, Paralyze, and other spells

When to Use Flaviria (UC)

Flaviria (UC) excels as a support trust who keeps the party buffed with Haste and Refresh while debuffing enemies. She can serve as a backup healer in a pinch. She pairs well with a dedicated healer and tank, filling the support role that makes the whole party more effective. Her Refresh is especially valuable for MP-hungry party members.
