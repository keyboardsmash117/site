Nashmeira

Melee / Healer Hybrid — Puppetmaster / White Mage

Who is Nashmeira?

Nashmeira is the Empress of the great eastern empire of Aht Urhgan. Despite her royal status, she is a capable combatant who fights alongside you during the TOAU storyline. She combines puppetmaster combat techniques with white magic healing for a versatile hybrid role.

What They Do

RoleMelee / Healer Hybrid
Main JobPuppetmaster (PUP) / White Mage (WHM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
How to ObtainComplete TOAU missions. Upgrade to Nashmeira II available via login points (pure healer).

Key Abilities

  • Melee Attacks — Engages in hand-to-hand combat alongside party members
  • Cure Magic — Heals party members with white magic between attacks
  • Automaton — Deploys a puppet companion that assists in combat
  • Status Buffs — Provides Protect and Shell to the party

When to Use Nashmeira

Nashmeira is a jack-of-all-trades who can fill both melee and healing roles simultaneously, though she excels at neither individually. She is useful when you need a flexible party member who can adapt to the situation. Her upgraded version, Nashmeira II, focuses entirely on healing if you need a dedicated support.
