Lhu Mhakaracca

Melee DD — Beastmaster

Who is Lhu Mhakaracca?

Lhu Mhakaracca is a Mithra Beastmaster from the Treasures of Aht Urhgan expansion. Beastmasters in FFXI are known for their ability to charm and command wild creatures, but as a Trust, Lhu fights on her own with savage ferocity. Her Warrior sub job gives her extra offensive punch.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD (damage dealer)
Main JobBeastmaster (BST)
Sub JobWarrior (WAR)
ExpansionTreasures of Aht Urhgan
Login Points Cost100 points (Set 3)

Key Abilities

  • Feral Howl — A powerful ability that inflicts multiple debuffs on the enemy, weakening it significantly. This can slow the target, lower its stats, and make the fight easier for everyone.
  • Berserk — From her Warrior sub job, this boosts her attack power at the cost of defense. Combined with her natural Beastmaster strength, it makes her hits count.
  • Axe Combat — Beastmasters wield axes, which are solid melee weapons with good damage per hit. She fights aggressively in melee range.
  • Reward — A Beastmaster ability that can heal pets. While she does not summon a pet as a Trust, her toolkit still includes useful self-buffs.

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Berserk, Aggressor, Feral Howl

When to Use Lhu Mhakaracca

Lhu Mhakaracca is a straightforward melee damage dealer with the bonus of Feral Howl's debuffs. She is a good pick when you want a Trust that contributes both damage and enemy weakening. Her WAR sub job makes her more offensively capable than you might expect from a Beastmaster. Use her alongside a tank and healer for a balanced party.
