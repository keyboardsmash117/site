Invincible Shield (UC)

Melee Damage Dealer — Warrior

Who is Invincible Shield?

Invincible Shield — also known as Iron Eater — is a proud Galka Warrior from Bastok. This Unity Concord version upgrades his AI and combat abilities significantly. Unity Concord trusts are enhanced variants obtained through the Unity system, offering better stats and smarter behavior than their base counterparts.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobWarrior (WAR)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionBastok
How to ObtainUnity Concord system — speak to a Unity NPC after joining a Unity faction

Key Abilities

  • Mighty Strikes — all attacks become critical hits for a duration
  • Warcry — attack boost for the party
  • Berserk — increases attack at the cost of defense
  • Defender — increases defense when needed
  • Improved AI — better weapon skill timing and ability usage compared to base Iron Eater

Ability Summary

  • Job abilities: Provoke, Warcry, Blood Rage, Restraint, Tomahawk
  • Tier-scaling stat: Damage Taken reduction (1.0× → 2.5×)

When to Use Invincible Shield (UC)

Invincible Shield is a straightforward melee damage dealer. His enhanced Warrior AI makes him hit harder and use abilities more effectively than the base version. He works well in any party that already has a tank and healer covered, adding raw physical damage output. A good pick when you want reliable melee damage without complication.
