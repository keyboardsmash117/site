Lilisette II

Melee DD — Dancer

Who is Lilisette II?

Lilisette is the heroine of the Wings of the Goddess expansion, a spirited Elvaan dancer who fights to change the course of history during the Crystal War. This upgraded version shifts her focus from support to offense, turning her dance moves into deadly attacks while still maintaining her self-sustaining nature.

What They Do

RoleMelee DD (damage dealer)

Key Abilities

  • Sambas — Dance moves that add bonus effects to melee attacks. Drain Samba steals HP with each hit, while Haste Samba speeds up attack rate. These affect the whole party if you are in range.
  • Waltzes — Healing dances that restore HP to herself or party members. Dancers use TP (the same gauge used for weapon skills) to heal, so they can keep fighting and healing at the same time.
  • Steps — Debuffing dances that weaken the enemy. Each step lowers the target's stats, making it easier for everyone to hit and harder for the enemy to fight back.
  • Self-Sustain — Lilisette II can heal herself through Drain Samba and Waltzes, meaning she barely needs outside healing. This makes her very low-maintenance.

When to Use Lilisette II

Lilisette II is excellent when you want a damage dealer who can take care of herself. Her Drain Samba keeps her HP topped off through normal attacks, and her Waltzes give her emergency healing. She is a great pick for situations where your healer is stretched thin, or when you want to run with fewer support Trusts and more damage. She pairs well with any tank.
