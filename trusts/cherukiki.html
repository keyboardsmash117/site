Cherukiki

Healer — White Mage

Who is Cherukiki?

Cherukiki is a Tarutaru White Mage from Windurst and the twin sister of Kupipi. She serves at the Optistery alongside her sister and shares the same gentle, caring nature. Together, the twins are among the most reliable healers in the Federation of Windurst.

Key Abilities

  • Cure spells — heals party members to keep everyone alive
  • Protect / Shell — buffs the party with defensive magic
  • Status removal — removes debuffs like Paralysis, Poison, and Blindness with -na spells

Ability Summary

  • Spells: Silence, Cure family, Regen family, Protectra family, Shellra family, Haste family, Paralyze family, Slow family
  • Passive: +5 Regen

When to Use Cherukiki

Cherukiki is functionally very similar to her twin sister Kupipi, providing reliable White Mage healing from early in the game. She is a great choice when you need a dedicated healer and pairs well with any tank trust. If you already have Kupipi, Cherukiki can serve as a second healer for tougher content where one healer is not enough.
