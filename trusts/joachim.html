Joachim

Support — Bard

Who is Joachim?

Joachim is an Elvaan Bard from San d'Oria who uses the power of song to support his allies. He is a skilled musician who appears in the San d'Oria storyline, using his melodies to inspire and strengthen those around him in battle.

What They Do

RoleSupport (buffer)
Main JobBard (BRD)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSan d'Oria (base game)
How to ObtainTrust quest (San d'Oria)

Key Abilities

  • March — increases the party's Haste effect, making everyone attack faster
  • Madrigal — boosts the party's Accuracy, helping attacks land more consistently
  • Minuet — increases the party's Attack power for higher damage per hit

When to Use Joachim

Joachim is an excellent support trust whose songs provide powerful party-wide buffs. Bard songs are some of the most impactful buffs in the game: March gives Haste (faster attacks), Madrigal gives Accuracy (attacks hit more often), and Minuet gives Attack (attacks hit harder). He is a top pick for any party that wants to maximize melee damage output, and pairs especially well with melee DD trusts.
