Balamor

Melee DD — Dark Knight

Who is Balamor?

Balamor is the dark antagonist of the Seekers of Adoulin storyline. A sinister figure who manipulates events from the shadows of Ulbuka, he wields dark magic and swordplay with deadly proficiency. As a trust, his villainous power is turned to your advantage.

What They Do

RoleMelee Damage Dealer
Main JobDark Knight (DRK)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
How to ObtainComplete SOA missions

Key Abilities

  • Blood Weapon — Melee attacks drain HP from enemies
  • Absorb Spells — Drains stats from enemies to buff himself
  • Dark Magic — Casts Drain and Aspir for self-sustain
  • Weapon Skills — Uses powerful great sword weapon skills for burst damage

Ability Summary

  • Spells: Absorb STR, Absorb DEX, Absorb VIT, Absorb AGI, Absorb INT, Absorb MND, Absorb CHR
  • Passive: +hpBoost HP, +100% max MP

When to Use Balamor

Balamor is a hard-hitting Dark Knight who combines melee damage with dark magic self-sustain. His Blood Weapon and Absorb spells let him stay healthy while dealing heavy damage. He is best used when you want a self-sufficient melee damage dealer who does not rely heavily on healer support.
