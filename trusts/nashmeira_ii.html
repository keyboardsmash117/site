Nashmeira II

Dedicated Healer — White Mage

Who is Nashmeira II?

Nashmeira is the Empress of the Empire of Aht Urhgan, a central figure in the Treasures of Aht Urhgan storyline. Despite her royal status, she fights alongside adventurers to protect her people. The original Nashmeira trust was a melee hybrid who tried to do a bit of everything. Nashmeira II is her upgraded version, redesigned as a dedicated healer — a significant improvement over the base version and a solid endgame healing option, though not necessarily the single "best" pick (Apururu (UC), Yoran-Oran (UC), and others are also strong choices depending on your party composition).

Key Abilities

  • Cure spells (Cure through Cure VI) — These are the bread-and-butter healing spells in FFXI. They restore HP (health points) to a party member. Higher tiers heal more HP but cost more MP (magic points, the resource used to cast spells).
  • Afflatus Solace — A special White Mage mode that enhances all healing magic. When active, Cure spells become more powerful and also grant a small damage-absorbing shield to the person healed. Nashmeira II keeps this active, which is a big part of why she heals so well.
  • Raise — Revives a fallen party member who has been knocked out (reduced to 0 HP). In FFXI, when you die you stay down until someone casts Raise on you or you choose to respawn at your home point. Having a trust with Raise means you can recover from mistakes without losing progress.
  • Protect and Shell — Defensive buffs. Protect reduces physical damage taken, Shell reduces magic damage taken. She casts these on the party automatically.
  • Haste — A buff that makes you attack faster and reduces spell recast times. Very valuable for damage dealers.

Tier Upgrades

Nashmeira II is one of the trusts with the custom Scythe tier-upgrade system. Each tier dramatically increases her toolkit — she starts solid and ends as a top-tier endgame healer.

When to Use Nashmeira II

A solid pick for most content at any tier — and at Tier III she becomes a self-sustaining endgame healer who buffs the party with Haste II and Refresh while keeping herself full-HP via Convert. Her Afflatus Solace + Cure Potency stacking makes her Cures hit harder than most alternatives.

For a typical party setup, summon her as your healer, then fill the remaining slots with a tank and damage dealers. She pairs well with any composition because she focuses entirely on keeping the party alive.

Alternatives worth trying: Apururu (UC) and Yoran-Oran (UC) are often favoured at endgame for their cure speed and Unity Concord upgrades. If you are still early in the game, use Karaha-Baruha or Cid as a placeholder healer until you can afford Set 5 from the Login Points vendor.
