Arciela

Magic Hybrid — Red Mage / Black Mage

Who is Arciela?

Arciela is the princess of the Sacred City of Adoulin and a central figure in the Seekers of Adoulin storyline. Despite her royal upbringing, she is a capable mage who ventures into the wilds of Ulbuka alongside adventurers. She wields both red and black magic to support her allies and punish her enemies.

What They Do

RoleMagic Hybrid
Main JobRed Mage (RDM) / Black Mage (BLM)
Sub JobNone
ExpansionSeekers of Adoulin
How to ObtainComplete SOA missions. Has upgrade Arciela II.

Key Abilities

  • Enfeebling Magic — Debuffs enemies with Slow, Paralyze, Silence, and more
  • Elemental Nukes — Casts offensive black magic for damage
  • Cure Magic — Provides backup healing when needed
  • Refresh/Haste — Buffs party with MP regeneration and attack speed

When to Use Arciela

Arciela is a versatile magic user who can debuff enemies, nuke, and provide light healing and buffing. She is a good fit when you want a mage who can do a bit of everything rather than specializing. For a stronger version with enhanced magic, consider upgrading to Arciela II.
