Weapon Progression

Streamlined paths to endgame weapons

Overview

Scythe offers streamlined progression paths to all five categories of endgame weapons. The retail grind has been adjusted to be achievable without spending months on a single weapon, while still requiring meaningful effort.

There are three ways to get started:

  • The !rema shop (skip the base-weapon grind) — once you meet the gate, buy a base Relic 75 / Mythic 75 / Empyrean 80 weapon for your current job at 5M gil each. Full detail below.
  • Traditional routes — Dynamis for Relic, Nyzul Isle / Einherjar / Assault for Mythic, Abyssea / Magian Trials for Empyrean, Escha for Aeonic, Odyssey for Prime.
  • Hunt shortcut Kupons — Kupon I-RME at 400 unique NM kills (upgrade materials) and Kupon W-RMEA at 500 (a completely free 119-III weapon of your choice). See the Scyld milestone page.

Step 0 — The !rema Shop

Getting a base REMA weapon on retail means months of farming currency and NM drops before you can even start upgrading. Scythe replaces that bottleneck with the !rema command — once you've proven yourself by rank-10 fame and the classic HNM gauntlet, you can buy the base weapon outright for 5 million gil.

How to unlock the shop
  1. Reach nation rank 10 on any of the three starting nations. This is the full Mission line (ZM→CoP is the usual path). Your current nation's rank is what counts — ally your rank-10 nation if you've switched.
  2. Kill each of the 10 gate HNMs at least once (listed below). Kills are credited per-character and persist forever. Alliance members in the same zone at time of death get credit too.
  3. Save 5,000,000 gil per weapon you intend to buy.
  4. Type !rema in chat. If the gate is open, the shop opens with every base REMA weapon available to your currently equipped main job. If not, the command prints your rank + kill progress so you can see what's left.

The 10 Gate HNMs

One kill each. These are the classic sky / sea / Abyssea-era Hyper NMs — none of the custom hunt NMs count. You can kill them in any order, solo or with a group.

Relic Weapons

Relic weapons are the original endgame weapons from Dynamis. On Scythe, all 14 relics have been rebalanced to be genuinely competitive (see Relic Weapons Reborn).

StageRequirement
Stage 1 (base)Obtain from Dynamis — currency + NM drops
Stage 2 → 3Ancient currency exchange (2 hour wait)
Stage 3 → 4Ancient currency exchange (1 hour wait)
Stage 4 → 119Riftcinder + Pluton
119 → 119-IIPluton (300 total)
119-II → 119-IIIPluton (3,000 total)

Scythe adjustments: Currency exchange rates and wait times are reduced. All 14 relic WSes buffed to fTP 5.0/9.0/13.0 with multi-hit fTP replication and OCC 35%/3x. Relic WSes are unlocked by weapon skill level — you do not need the relic equipped to use them. All tiers (75, 119, 119-III) have custom stat buffs.

Oboro & Forge Cache (Port Jeuno): Deposit upgrade materials (Pluton, Beitetsu, Riftborn, Riftcinder, Astral Detritus) into the Forge Cache NPC. Trade your 99 AG weapon to Oboro to upgrade it. Stage 1 draws 300 materials from storage. Stage 2 to 119-III requires 10,000 materials + Scintillating Rhapsody. RP augmentation available with Astral Detritus.

All 14 relics: Spharai, Mandau, Excalibur, Ragnarok, Guttler, Bravura, Gungnir, Apocalypse, Kikoku, Amanomurakumo, Mjollnir, Claustrum, Yoichinoyumi, Annihilator.

The Hunt Shortcut

Don't want to grind the traditional progression? The NM Hunt milestone system offers an alternative path:

MilestoneKuponScyldWhat You Get
400 unique killsKupon I-RME3,500RME weapon upgrade item
500 unique killsKupon W-RMEA7,500Any RMEA weapon of your choice

Killing 500 unique NMs requires completing the entire regular hunt (475 NMs) plus 25 elite bosses. This is the longest-term goal on the server, but it guarantees the weapon of your choice without touching any of the traditional upgrade systems.
