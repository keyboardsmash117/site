Weapon Progression

Streamlined paths to endgame weapons

Overview

Scythe offers streamlined progression paths to all five categories of endgame weapons. The retail grind has been adjusted to be achievable without spending months on a single weapon, while still requiring meaningful effort and engagement with the game's content.

There are also two shortcut paths through the hunt system:

  • Kupon I-RME — available at 400 unique NM kills (3,500 Scyld). Provides an RME weapon upgrade item.
  • Kupon W-RMEA — available at 500 unique NM kills (7,500 Scyld). Choose any Relic, Mythic, Empyrean, or Aeonic weapon outright.

Relic Weapons

Relic weapons are the original endgame weapons from Dynamis. On Scythe, all 14 relics have been rebalanced to be genuinely competitive (see Relic Weapons Reborn).

StageRequirement
Stage 1 (base)Obtain from Dynamis — currency + NM drops
Stage 2 → 3Ancient currency exchange (2 hour wait)
Stage 3 → 4Ancient currency exchange (1 hour wait)
Stage 4 → 119Riftcinder + Pluton
119 → 119-IIPluton (300 total)
119-II → 119-IIIPluton (3,000 total)

Scythe adjustments: Currency exchange rates and wait times are reduced. All 14 relic WSes buffed to fTP 5.0/9.0/13.0 with multi-hit fTP replication and OCC 35%/3x. Relic WSes are unlocked by weapon skill level — you do not need the relic equipped to use them. All tiers (75, 119, 119-III) have custom stat buffs.

Oboro & Forge Cache (Port Jeuno): Deposit upgrade materials (Pluton, Beitetsu, Riftborn, Riftcinder, Astral Detritus) into the Forge Cache NPC. Trade your 99 AG weapon to Oboro to upgrade it. Stage 1 draws 300 materials from storage. Stage 2 to 119-III requires 10,000 materials + Scintillating Rhapsody. RP augmentation available with Astral Detritus.

All 14 relics: Spharai, Mandau, Excalibur, Ragnarok, Guttler, Bravura, Gungnir, Apocalypse, Kikoku, Amanomurakumo, Mjollnir, Claustrum, Yoichinoyumi, Annihilator.

Mythic Weapons

Mythic weapons are obtained through Assault, Nyzul Isle, and Einherjar content from Treasures of Aht Urhgan.

StageRequirement
Base weaponNyzul Isle Investigation (floor 100)
Upgrade pathAlexandrite + Einherjar + Assault points
119 upgradeRiftborn Boulder
119-II → 119-IIIRiftborn Boulder (3,000 total)

Scythe adjustments: Alexandrite is available from the Scyld milestone shop (25 Scyld each). Nyzul Isle casket drops and Vigil weapon drops are enabled.

Empyrean Weapons

Empyrean weapons come from Abyssea and the Trial of the Magians system.

StageRequirement
Base weaponMagian Trial base weapon
Trial stagesMagian Trials (retail requirements)
119 upgradeBeitetsu
119-II → 119-IIIBeitetsu (3,000 total)

Scythe adjustments: Magian trial requirements have been streamlined — reduced kill counts, adjusted conditions. Beitetsu is available from the Scyld milestone shop. Empyrean aftermath procs on weapon skills (not just auto-attacks).

Aeonic Weapons

Aeonic weapons are obtained through Escha content and the Sacred Kindred's Crest quest line from Seekers of Adoulin.

StageRequirement
Quest startObtain Sacred Kindred's Crest (Escha - Zi'Tah)
Boss fightsDefeat Escha NMs for key items
Final weaponTrade KIs + crest to obtain Aeonic

Scythe adjustments: Aeonic weapons are obtainable through the standard Escha quest chain. Also available via Kupon W-RMEA from the NM Hunt milestone system at 500 unique kills.

Prime Weapons

Prime weapons are the newest endgame weapon category, obtainable through Odyssey content.

StageRequirement
Base weaponOdyssey Sheol Gaol clears
Upgrade pathOdyssey materials + NM drops

Scythe adjustments: Prime weapon progression is available through Odyssey content. Specific adjustments to material requirements and clear conditions may apply — check Discord for current details.

The Hunt Shortcut

Don't want to grind the traditional progression? The NM Hunt milestone system offers an alternative path:

MilestoneKuponScyldWhat You Get
400 unique killsKupon I-RME3,500RME weapon upgrade item
500 unique killsKupon W-RMEA7,500Any RMEA weapon of your choice

Killing 500 unique NMs requires completing the entire regular hunt (475 NMs) plus 25 elite bosses. This is the longest-term goal on the server, but it guarantees the weapon of your choice without touching any of the traditional upgrade systems.
