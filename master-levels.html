Master Levels

50 levels of post-cap progression

50 master levels earned by accumulating Exemplar Points from Colosseum fights. Master levels are tracked per-job — your WAR master level is separate from your BLM master level.

  • Level 0-10: 2,500 - 31,147 EP per level
  • Level 10-20: 41,205 - 85,775 EP per level
  • Level 20-30: 109,112 - 205,578 EP per level
  • Level 30-40: 258,409 - 606,296 EP per level
  • Level 40-50: 769,426 - 3,354,604 EP per level

Higher master levels unlock higher Colosseum tiers. Adamant tier (the hardest) requires ML 50 and pits you against level 150 bosses with 8 million HP.
