The Colosseum

Timed arena boss fights with 5 tiers and 5 modes

Coming Soon The Colosseum is in active development and not yet available on the live server. The design below documents the intended system — tiers, rewards, and modes may change before launch.

A custom arena zone with timed boss fights. When it launches, the Colosseum will be the primary source of Exemplar Points (EP) — the currency used for Master Levels. The design below documents the intended system; values are subject to change before release.

Until it launches — where EP comes from

The Colosseum itself is not yet playable. Right now, Exemplar Points come from:

  • Siege raids — server-wide 5-wave events awarding EP on participation. Watch linkshell chat for the siege-start announcement.
  • Boosted Abyssea NMs — selected Abyssea NMs award bonus EP alongside retail Abyssea rewards. See the Other Features page.

When the Colosseum ships it slots in alongside these as the highest-throughput EP source. Your Master Level progress carries forward — no reset.

Each tier holds multiple unique boss fights with their own mechanics. Boss level and HP scale with tier. EP rewards above are the base value — challenge modes multiply.

5 Challenge Modes

  • Normal — standard fight, up to 6 players
  • Hard — 1.5x HP, 1.3x stats, extra adds spawn, 1.5x EP reward
  • Speed Kill — 0.8x HP but only 40% of the time limit, 2x EP reward
  • Solo — 0.5x HP, solo only, 2.5x EP reward
  • No Magic — no spells allowed, 0.7x HP, 1.8x EP reward

Gauntlet mode available — fight consecutive bosses within a tier, ascending through tiers, or endurance runs. Gauntlet rewards scale with streak length.
